tvilling (12933)


Etymology

From Old Swedish tvinlinger, possibly from a Old Norse root *tvinlingr, from Proto-Germanic *twinilingaz, with the first element from *twinaz. Compare Norwegian and Danish tvilling, English twinling, Dutch tweeling, German Zwilling.

Noun

tvilling c

  1. twin (either of two people who shared the same uterus at the same time, or of two similar or closely related objects)
