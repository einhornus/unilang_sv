starta (1293)


Verb

starta (present startar, preterite startade, supine startat, imperative starta)

  1. to begin, to start; to get something going; as in, to start an engine, to start a trip, to start a race; to start a company
    Starta motorerna, vi lyfter genast!
    Fire up the engines, we will take off immediately!
