äcklig (5756)


Etymology

From äckel + -lig. Cognate with German eklig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɛklɪ(ɡ)/

Adjective

äcklig (comparative äckligare, superlative äckligast)

  1. disgusting, icky
  2. (of food) distasteful, not tasty
    Antonym: god

Related terms

  • äckel
  • äckla
