agenda (14202)


Etymology

From Latin agenda (“what ought to be done”).

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

agenda c

  1. an agenda

Usage notes

  • The agenda for a meeting is normally called dagordning. The word agenda is more often used in the abstract sense of somebody's "political agenda".
