genomsnitt (21116)


Etymology

Calque of German Durchschnitt, from durch + Schnitt, equivalent to genom (“through”) + snitt (“cut”). Cognate with Danish gennemsnit, Norwegian Bokmål gjennomsnitt and Norwegian Nynorsk gjennomsnitt.

Noun

genomsnitt n

  1. average
