svalla (76775)


Etymology

svall + -a

Verb

svalla (present svallar, preterite svallade, supine svallat, imperative svalla)

  1. to swell, to surge (of the motion of waves on a shore)
  2. to undulate, to billow, to heave (to move like a wave, up and down)
  3. to seethe
  4. to effervesce
  5. to flush, to flow (of blood or words in speech)
  6. to run high, to overflow (of emotions)
