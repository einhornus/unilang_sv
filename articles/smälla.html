smälla (11475)


Noun

smälla c

  1. a flyswatter
  2. something that has a banging sound

Verb

smälla (present smäller, preterite smällde or small, supine smällt or smullit, imperative smäll)

  1. to whip, to hit, to slam, to bang, to smack, to knock, to crack (either to hit, or to sound as if hitting)
    smäll inte i dörren!
    don't slam the door!

Usage notes

  • The strong conjugation is used in past tense for the intransitive case (skottet small), but the supine is less common.
