bokstav (14970)


Etymology

From Old Swedish bōkstaver, from Old Norse bókstafr, from Proto-Germanic *bōkastabaz. Compare English bookstave.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbuːkˌstɑːv/, /ˈbʊkˌstɑːv/

Noun

bokstav c
