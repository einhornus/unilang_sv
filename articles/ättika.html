ättika (60603)


Etymology

From late Old Norse edik, from Middle Low German etik or Middle Dutch edik, ultimately borrowed (with metathesis) from Latin acētum. Compare Faroese edikur, Norwegian eddik, Danish eddike, Icelandic edik, Old English æced, German Essig.

Noun

ättika c

  1. vinegar (diluted acetic acid)
