leverera (4544)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

leverera (present levererar, preterite levererade, supine levererat, imperative leverera)

  1. to deliver; to transport something on someone's behalf
  2. to deliver; to produce the wished-for result
    Lagets nyinköpte målskytt har hittills misslyckats med att leverera vad fansen har önskat sig.
    The newly bought scorer of the team has so far failed to deliver what the fans have wished for.
