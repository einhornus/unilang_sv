skaffa (787)


Etymology

From Old Swedish skaffa, from Middle High German schaffen, from Old High German scaffan (from scepfen, cognate with Old Swedish skepja (see Swedish skapa)).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

skaffa (present skaffar, preterite skaffade, supine skaffat, imperative skaffa)

  1. to get, to acquire, to procure
