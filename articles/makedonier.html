makedonier (73642)


Etymology

From Latin mācedonus, mācedonicus, from Ancient Greek μακεδόνιος (makedónios), from μακεδών (makedṓn) + -ιος (-ios), equivalent to Makedonien + -ier.

Noun

makedonier c

  1. a Macedonian (person from the Republic of Macedonia)

Related terms

  • Republiken Makedonien, Makedonien
  • makedonisk / makedonsk
  • makedonska / makedoniska (rare)
