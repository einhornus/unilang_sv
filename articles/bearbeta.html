bearbeta (16594)


Etymology

From German bearbeiten or Low German bearbeiden. Equivalent of be- + arbeta. Cognate with Danish bearbejde, Norwegian Bokmål bearbeide and Dutch bearbeiden.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbeːarˌbeːta/

Verb

bearbeta (present bearbetar, preterite bearbetade, supine bearbetat, imperative bearbeta)

  1. to work (shape, form, or improve a material)
