gärd (117216)


Etymology

From Old Swedish gærþ (“a deed, a cost, what must be done”), from Old Norse gørð, from Proto-Germanic *garwidō. Related to göra (“to do”). Compare Icelandic gerð, Danish gærd.

Noun

gärd c

  1. (dated) a deed, an action, a cost
