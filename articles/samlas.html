samlas (3712)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

samlas (present samlas, preterite samlades, supine samlats, imperative samlas)

  1. (deponent, reciprocal) to gather, to meet
    Vi samlas här klockan tre.
    We are meeting here at three o'clock.
  2. infinitive passive of samla.
  3. present tense passive of samla.
