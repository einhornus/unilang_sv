förkläde (13460)


Etymology

för + kläde

Noun

förkläde n

  1. apron; An article of clothing worn over the front of the torso for protection from spills.
  2. (dated) chaperon, a person who follows, and is supposed to protect the honour of a young woman when meeting men.
