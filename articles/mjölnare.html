mjölnare (105574)


Etymology

Old Swedish mölna, mylna (“mill”) (from Latin molīna). Equivalent to mjöl + -are. Alternatively directly from Late Latin molīnārius.

Spelling influenced by mjöl (“flour, meal”), which shares a common Proto-Indo-European etymon.

Noun

mjölnare c

  1. a miller (person)
