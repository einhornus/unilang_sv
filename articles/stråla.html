stråla (12822)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɾoːˌla/

Verb

stråla (present strålar, preterite strålade, supine strålat, imperative stråla)

  1. to radiate; to emit rays (also in a figurative sense)
  2. to radiate; to come out as rays
  3. to radiate; to expose to ionizing radiation
  4. to shine very brightly; to beam
  5. to beam; to smile broadly
