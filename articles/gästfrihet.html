gästfrihet (10718)


Etymology

From gästfri (“hospitable”, adjective) + -het (noun-forming suffix). Compare Danish gæstfrihed, Dutch gastvrijheid.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jɛstfriːheːt/
