eller (63)


Etymology

From Old Swedish ælla, ællar, from Old Norse ella, ellar, from Proto-Germanic *aljaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ályos.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛlɛr/, [ˈɛlːær]
  • audio
    		(file)

Conjunction

eller

  1. or
  2. nor
    Han sade varken ja eller nej.
    He said neither yes nor no.
