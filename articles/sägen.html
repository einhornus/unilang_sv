sägen (26485)


Etymology

From Old Swedish säghn, saghn, from Old Norse sǫgn, from Proto-Germanic *sagnō, cognate with Danish sagn, Old English sæġen (“saying, statement”). Derived from *sagjaną (“to say”).

Noun

sägen c

  1. a tale, a hearsay, a story, a fairytale
