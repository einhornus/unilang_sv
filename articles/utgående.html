utgående (22020)


Etymology

present participle of utgå, literally: going out

Adjective

utgående

  1. outwards, outward bound, departing
  2. discontinued, expiring
    realisation på utgående varor
    sale of discontinued products
    att vara på utgående
    about to be discontinued
  3. based on
    rösträtten bestäms utgående från ...
    the right to vote is decided based on ...

Verb

utgående

  1. present participle of utgå; starting (from), based (on)
