greja (17709)


Etymology 1

From Old Norse greiða. See also Norwegian Nynorsk greia, Norwegian greie

Verb

greja (present grejar, preterite grejade, supine grejat, imperative greja)

  1. to pull off, manage, handle, fix
    Tror du hon kommer greja det här?
    Do you think she will pull this off?
    Jag grejar det!
    I'll fix it!
