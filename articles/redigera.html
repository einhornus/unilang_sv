redigera (42473)


Etymology

From Latin redigere ("bring to order"; "bring together"), from red- ("again", "anew"; "backward"; ancient form of re-) and agere ("do", "act"). [1]

Verb

redigera (present redigerar, preterite redigerade, supine redigerat, imperative redigera)

  1. to edit; to make changes to a text or a document
    Jag redigerade den första scenen i filmen, så att den blev mer effektfull.
    I edited the first scene in the film to make it more effective.
