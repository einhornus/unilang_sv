ledare (1571)


Etymology

leda + -are

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

ledare

  1. comparative degree of led

Noun

ledare c

  1. a leader; ruler
  2. someone who leads, guides or directs somebody else
  3. the competitor who is in the lead in a race
  4. conductor; material which can conduct electricity well (less commonly: heat)
  5. an editorial article, short for ledarartikel
