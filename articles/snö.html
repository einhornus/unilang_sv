snö (3935)


Etymology

From Old Swedish *sniōr, from Old Norse snær, from Proto-Germanic *snaiwaz, from Proto-Indo-European *snóygʷʰos.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /snøː/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -øː

Noun

snö c (uncountable)

  1. snow; frozen, crystalline water falling as precipitation
  2. snow; random electrical noise in a television picture.
  3. (slang) cocaine
