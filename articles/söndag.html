söndag (2107)


Etymology

The word is originally a short form for solens dag (day of the sun). From Old Swedish sunnodagher, from Old Norse sunnudagr, from late Proto-Germanic *sunnōniz dagaz, a calque of Latin diēs Sōlis.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsœndɑːɡ/, /ˈsœnda/
  • audio

Noun

söndag c

  1. Sunday. The seventh day of the week (according to the ISO 8601 standard).
