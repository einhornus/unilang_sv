skicka (544)


Etymology

From Middle Low German schicken "to send" (German schicken, Dutch schikken). Cognate with ske "to happen, to occur"

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɧɪkˌa/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

skicka (present skickar, preterite skickade, supine skickat, imperative skicka)

  1. send (make something go somewhere)
    Jag skickade brevet igår.
    I sent the letter yesterday.
  2. pass, to give someone who didn't reach (e.g. across a table)
    Kan du skicka saltet?
    Could you please pass the salt?
  3. (reflexive, dated) behave oneself
