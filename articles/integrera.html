integrera (53338)


Verb

integrera (present integrerar, preterite integrerade, supine integrerat, imperative integrera)

  1. to integrate, to adopt, to make a part fit in a whole, to blend in
  2. (for immigrants) to adopt to a new country
  3. (mathematics) to integrate (to subject to the operation of integration; to find the integral of)

Usage notes

  • Swedish governments 1954-1994 had a Minister of Immigration (invandrarminister), belonging to the Ministry of Labour. From 1996, when post-war labour immigration was no longer at hand and Sweden had joined the European Union, this was replaced with a Minister of Migration (migrationsminister) and a Minister of Integration (integrationsminister), both belonging to the Ministry of Justice. During 1998-2007 a separate Integration Agency (integrationsverket) also existed.
