kul (381)


Etymology

Of unclear origin. Perhaps a clipping from an earlier kulan, from kula. First seen in 1911.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kʉːl/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ʉːl

Adjective

kul

  1. fun, nice, enjoyable, amusing

Declension

No inflected forms. Sometimes kulig is used when inflected forms are needed.

Related terms

  • kulig
  • skitkul
