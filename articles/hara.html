hara (14812)


Etymology

From hare + -a

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /hɑːra/

Verb

hara (present harar, preterite harade, supine harat, imperative hara)

  1. to trot; to move in a way that reminds the movement of a hare
    Nu ska jag hara iväg till affären. ― I'll trot to the store now.
