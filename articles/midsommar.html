midsommar (28419)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²mɪˌsɔmar/
  • (spelling pronunciation) IPA(key): /²miːdˌsɔmar/, /²mɪdˌsɔmar/

Noun

midsommar c

  1. midsummer

Usage notes

  • Midsommar can either be pronounced a short first vowel and no /d/, or with (usually) a long first vowel and an audible /d/. The first pronunciation is older and is an example of complete regressive assimilation, where clusters such as -ds- and -ts- were assimilated to -ss-, which also found in the pronunciation of words like ledsen, låtsas, matsäck, skjutsa, and many others. The second pronunciation is newer and have a reintroduced /d/ due to spelling pronunciation. Both are considered standard and correct.
