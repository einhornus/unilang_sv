lopp (5672)


Etymology

Related to the verb löpa, corresponding to German laufen and English leap.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lɔpː/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

lopp n

  1. a run, trace, track, path, a running (skiing, bicycling etc.) competition, a heat in a competition, the course of a run
