ridå (29025)


Etymology

Borrowed from French rideau, used in Swedish since 1787.

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -oː

Noun

ridå c

  1. a curtain (on a theatre)
    för öppen ridå ― in open daylight, in public view
    ridån går upp ― the play begins
    ridå! ― the end! (the end of an act, when the curtain comes down)
