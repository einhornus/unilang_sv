snickarverkstad (120360)


Etymology

snickare + verkstad, used since 1723

Noun

snickarverkstad c

  1. a carpentry, a carpenter's workshop

Usage notes

  • The trailing -d is most often silent when spoken, and the alternative definite singular form snickarverkstan is common.
