inleda (14802)


Etymology

From in- + leda.

Verb

inleda (present inleder, preterite inledde, supine inlett, imperative inled)

  1. begin, introduce, open
    Boken inleds med ett kapitel om papperstillverkningens historia.
    The book is introduced by a chapter on the history of paper making.
