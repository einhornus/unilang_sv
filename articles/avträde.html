avträde (117122)


Etymology

A noun based on the verb avträda (to leave, to depart), cognate with Middle Low German aftrede. Used in the sense of a private place for retreat (i.e. an outhouse), since 1730. In order to avoid misunderstandings, the other senses have become less common and eventually obsolete.

Noun

avträde n

  1. an outhouse
  2. (obsolete) a departure (from service or ownership), a retreat (from a battle), a damage (a payment for ending a conflict)
