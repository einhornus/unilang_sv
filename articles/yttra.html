yttra (15703)


Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

yttra (present yttrar, preterite yttrade, supine yttrat, imperative yttra)

  1. to utter, to say (transitive or reflexive)
  2. (reflexive) to manifest, to appear, to show (mostly with "sig som")
    Sjukdomen yttrade sig som blåsor i hud och slemhinnor.
    The disease was manifested as blisters on the skin and mucous membranes.
