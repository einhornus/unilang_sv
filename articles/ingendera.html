ingendera (35578)


Etymology

From ingen (“nobody”) + -dera, from Old Swedish þēra (“their”), genitive plural of þæn (“it”). Compare modern Swedish deras (“their”) and Icelandic þeirra (“their”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɪŋɛndeːra/

Pronoun

ingendera c (neuter ingetdera)

  1. neither (of two)
  2. none (of multiple things)

Related terms

  • endera
  • någondera
