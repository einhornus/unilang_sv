fånig (6454)


Etymology

fån (“halfwit”) + -ig

Adjective

fånig (comparative fånigare, superlative fånigast)

  1. (informal) silly, ridiculous
    Synonyms: fjantig, befängd, tramsig
  2. (informal) dumb, stupid
