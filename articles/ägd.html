ägd (28417)


Etymology

past participle of äga.

Adjective

ägd

  1. owned
    • 1861 Carl August Hagberg, Swedish translation of Shakespeare's Henry Vi, part 3, act 3, scene 1
      ty förnöjsamhet den kronan heter, sällan ägd af kungar.
      my crown is called content: A crown it is that seldom kings enjoy.

Usage notes

  • Since the 1990s also in the computer hacker sense of the word
