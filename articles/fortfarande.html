fortfarande (219)


Etymology

present participle of fortfara

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

fortfarande

  1. (dated) continuing, continuous, ongoing
    vexelströmmar eller alternerande strömmar, där en fortfarande omkastning i de alstrade strömmarnes riktning eger rum
    alternating currents, where a continuous reversal of the direction of the generated currents take place
    de rätta christna skola hafwa en fortfarande syndakänsla
    the righteous Christians shall have a continuous feeling of sin
    Sjelfve den fridsamme Lehnberg kan ej återhålla en uppmaning till fortfarande bardalek.
    Even the peaceful Lehnberg can not restrain an exhortation to continued bardship.

Adverb

fortfarande (not comparable)

  1. still; up to a time, as in the preceding time
    Konungens bevågenhet var fortfarande oförminskad.
    The king's good will was still undiminished.

Synonyms

  • ännu
  • alltjämt
