fusk (9342)


Etymology

From fuska (“to cheat”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fɵsk/
  • Rhymes: -ɵsk

Noun

fusk n

  1. cheating (the act of using illegal aids in order to gain an advantage in a test or competition, etc.)
    Synonym: fubb (colloquial)
  2. fraud, swindle
    valfusk ― electoral fraud
    skattefusk ― tax evasion
    Synonym: bedrägeri
  3. (in compounds) fake, simulated, faux
    fuskpäls ― fake fur
    fuskläder ― fake leather
  4. (usually in compounds) bad workmanship, shoddy work
    fuskbygge ― jerry-built construction
    fuskverk ― shoddy piece of work
