slockna (24446)


Etymology

From Old Norse slokna

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [²ˈslɔkːˌna]

Verb

slockna (present slocknar, preterite slocknade, supine slocknat, imperative slockna)

  1. (intransitive) to turn off
  2. to go out (of a light)
  3. to burn out
