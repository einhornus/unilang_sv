framkalla (14864)


Etymology

fram + kalla

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

framkalla (present framkallar, preterite framkallade, supine framkallat, imperative framkalla)

  1. to summon (a witness in a court)
  2. to cause
  3. to develop, to process (photographic images and film)

Usage notes

  • in the first sense, the verb can be separated (kalla fram)
  • see the present participle framkallande (“causing”) for compounds
