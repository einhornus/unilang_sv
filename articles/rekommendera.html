rekommendera (7259)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /rɛkʊmɛnˈdeːra/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

rekommendera (present rekommenderar, preterite rekommenderade, supine rekommenderat, imperative rekommendera)

  1. to recommend (to commend to the favorable notice of another)
  2. to register (a letter); see also rek
  3. (reflexive) to bid farewell
    Jag ska be att få rekommendera mig.
    I shall have to leave now.
    grevinnan rekommenderade sig och gick mot dörren
    the countess bade farewell and went towards the door
