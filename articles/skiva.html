skiva (5742)


Etymology

From Old Norse skífa.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

skiva c

  1. a leaf (flat section used to extend a table)
  2. a slice; a thin, broad piece cut off
  3. a disc; a thin, flat, circular plate or something resembling this
  4. (music) a record, such as a CD or a LP
  5. a party; social gathering
