justera (14961)


Etymology

From Medieval Latin justare.

Verb

justera (present justerar, preterite justerade, supine justerat, imperative justera)

  1. to adjust, to modify
  2. to verify the protocol of a meeting
  3. (typography) to align (text to margins)
  4. (colloquial) to injure someone
