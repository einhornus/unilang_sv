byxa (59549)


Etymology

From Middle Low German buxe, cognate with Icelandic buxur, Danish buks, Norwegian bukse, Norwegian Nynorsk bukse, Kashubian bùksë.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

byxa c

  1. pants, trousers

Usage notes

In most circumstances the plural will be used. One sees the singular form mainly in advertisements.
