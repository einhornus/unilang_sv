flintis (22891)


Etymology 1

From flint or clipping of flintskallig + -is.

Adjective

flintis (comparative mer flintis, superlative mest flintis)

  1. (colloquial) bald
    Synonyms: flint, flintskallig

Declension

Invariable, not used in the definite form.

Etymology 2

From flint or clipping of flintskalle + -is.

Noun

flintis c

  1. (colloquial) a bald person, a baldie
    Synonym: flintskalle
