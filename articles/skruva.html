skruva (5850)


Etymology

skruv + -a

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

skruva (present skruvar, preterite skruvade, supine skruvat, imperative skruva)

  1. to screw; to fasten (or unfasten) a screw
  2. to rotate something along its length axis
  3. (ball sports) to give spin to the ball
  4. (reflexive) to twist and turn; to display discomfort; to squirm
