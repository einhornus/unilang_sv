rast (5820)


Etymology

From Old Norse rǫst, from Proto-Germanic *rastō, *rastijō (“rest”), from Proto-Indo-European *ros-, *res-, *ErH- (“rest”).

Noun

rast c

  1. a break (rest or pause, usually from work)
