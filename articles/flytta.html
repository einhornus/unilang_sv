flytta (529)


Etymology

From Old Swedish flytia, from Old Norse flytja, from Proto-Germanic *flutjaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /flʏtːa/
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

flytta (present flyttar, preterite flyttade, supine flyttat, imperative flytta)

  1. to move; to relocate an object
  2. to move; to change one's place of living
  3. to make a move in certain board games, such as chess
