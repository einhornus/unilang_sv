givare (21334)


Etymology

giva (“obsolete form of ge”) + -are.

Noun

givare c

  1. a giver (someone who gives, rather than takes)
    Man måste inte vara antingen givare eller tagare.
    You need not be either a giver or a taker.
  2. a giver, a donor (someone who donates money to charities)
    Gud älskar en glad givare.
    God loveth a cheerful giver. (2 Corinthians 9:7)
  3. a sensor (a device that measures and gives information)
  4. a provider, a supplier (rather than recipient, customer)
