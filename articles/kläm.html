kläm (9186)


Noun

kläm c

  1. a squeeze; a narrow passage or a difficult situation
    sitta i kläm
    be in a squeeze, be stuck
    hamna i kläm
    get in a squeeze, get stuck
  2. energy, emphasis, force, skill; med kläm
  3. a point, a punch line of a joke (a final emphasis); also slutkläm
  4. a short summary
  5. (in Finland) a petition to parliament or a municipal assembly
