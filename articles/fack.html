fack (13165)


Pronunciation

  • audio

Noun

fack n

  1. a compartment, a box, a slot (one of several)
  2. a trade, a profession, a subject of expertise (seen as a compartment of the larger work life)
  3. a trade union; Clipping of fackförening.
