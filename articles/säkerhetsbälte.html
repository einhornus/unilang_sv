säkerhetsbälte (33553)


Etymology

säkerhet + bälte, used since 1920 in this sense (since 1879 in a different, now obsolete definition of a geographic zone)

Noun

säkerhetsbälte n

  1. seat belt (restraining belt), safety belt
    använd säkerhetsbältet!
    buckle up! (literally: use [your] seat belt)
