blask (98059)


Etymology

Likely onomatopoeic, compare words for ‘splash,’ Swedish blaska, plaska, Norwegian blaskra, blaske, blasse, Middle High German blatzen, Scots blash.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²bɽæsk/
    Rhymes: -àsk

Verb

blask

  1. To blink, wink.[1][2][3][4][5]

Synonyms

  • blysch, blyskh
