lycka (624)


Etymology 1

From Middle Low German lucke, gelucke. According to the OED, it may be related to the source of English lock (compare Proto-Germanic *lūkaną (“to close, to shut”)).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /lʏkːa/

Noun

lycka c

  1. joy, happiness
  2. (archaic) a loop (of string)
  3. a small farm field
  4. luck (something that happens to someone by chance), fortune (good or bad), fate
