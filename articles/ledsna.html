ledsna (5425)


Etymology

From ledsen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²lɛsna/
  • Rhymes: -ɛsna

Adjective

ledsna

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of ledsen.

Verb

ledsna (present ledsnar, preterite ledsnade, supine ledsnat, imperative ledsna)

  1. (intransitive, of activities) to become tired and bored
    Jag börjar ledsna på det här.
    I'm starting to get tired of this.
