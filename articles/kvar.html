kvar (236)


Etymology

From Old Norse kvirr, kyrr, from Proto-Germanic *kwerruz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kvɑːr/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

kvar

  1. left, remaining
    Det är två kakor kvar
    There are two remaining cookies

Usage notes

Often as vara kvar: "(to) remain", "(to) be left", or as bli kvar: "be left behind", "(to) stay behind"
