borgare (74463)


Etymology

borg + -are, like German Bürger

Noun

borgare c

  1. a member of the bourgeoisie, a town-dweller, a citizen, one of the 4 estates (nobility, priests, bourgeoisie, peasants)
  2. a nonsocialist politician or voter
