fission (117779)


Etymology

Borrowed from English fission, from Latin fissiō, fissiōnem.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fɪˈɧuːn/

Noun

fission c

  1. (physics) nuclear fission
  2. The process whereby a company splits to become two.
