engelsk (5250)


Etymology

From Old Norse engilskr, enskr, "English", from Old English Englisc, from engle, angle, "Angles".

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

engelsk

  1. English; of or pertaining to England. Commonly used instead of brittisk (of or pertaining to Great Britain).
