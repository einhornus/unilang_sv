apotek (14102)


Etymology

From Latin apotheca (“storehouse”), from Ancient Greek ἀποθήκη (apothḗkē, “storehouse”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /apʊˈteːk/

Noun

apotek n

  1. pharmacy (a place where prescription drugs are dispensed)
  2. a small supply of first aid and common pharmaceuticals kept at home
    Synonyms: hemapotek, husapotek
  3. a condiment set, cruet-stand
    Synonym: bordsapotek
