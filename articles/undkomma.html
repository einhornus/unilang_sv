undkomma (13348)


Verb

undkomma (present undkommer, preterite undkom, supine undkommit, imperative undkom)

  1. to avoid, to escape
    En båtbrand undkoms som tur var och nödraketen kunde till sist avfyras.
    A boat fire was avoided fortunately and the signal rocket could at last be shot.
    Grefve Hård var undkommen och i säkerhet
    Count Hård had escaped and was in safety
