koll (2230)


Noun

koll c

  1. (colloquial) a look, a check, an inspection; Clipping of kontroll.
  2. (colloquial, uncountable) control (to have, to be in), clue, knowledge (of current trends)
    Han har noll koll.
    He is clueless.
