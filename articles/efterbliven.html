efterbliven (6866)


Etymology

From efter (“after”) + bliven (“having become”).

Adjective

efterbliven (comparative mer efterbliven, superlative mest efterbliven)

  1. (derogatory) retarded; with a mental retardation; underdeveloped; dumb
  2. (derogatory) backwards; behind current trends
