protestera (9354)


Etymology

protest + -era

Verb

protestera (present protesterar, preterite protesterade, supine protesterat, imperative protestera)

  1. to protest (to make a strong objection)

Usage notes

  • Swedish has a word protestant (“a protestant Christian person”), but none for protester (participant in a street protest). These can instead be called demonstrant, since demonstration is a synonym to street protest.
