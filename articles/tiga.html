tiga (7602)


Etymology

From Old Swedish þighia, from Old Norse þegja, from Proto-Germanic *þagjaną.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

tiga (present tiger, preterite teg, supine tigit, imperative tig)

  1. to keep quiet, to say absolutely nothing

Conjugation

An archaic supine is tegat.
