votera (296653)


Etymology

From English vote, used in Swedish since 1629.[1]

Verb

votera (present voterar, preterite voterade, supine voterat, imperative votera)

  1. (dated) to vote (in a particular question), to decide (a question) by voting, to grant (an amount of funding by voting), to elect (someone by voting)
    • "Röstsedeln" (1892), poem by Carl Snoilsky
      Kanske om Sverges väl och ve voteras re’n i natt.
      Perhaps of Sweden's weal and woe is voted already tonight.
    Synonym: rösta
