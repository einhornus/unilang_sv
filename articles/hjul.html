hjul (6240)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hiul, hiughl, from Old Norse hvél, hjól, from Proto-Germanic *hwehwlą, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷekʷlóm, *kʷékʷlos (“wheel”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jʉːl/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Homophones: jul
  • Rhymes: -ʉːl

Noun

hjul n

  1. a wheel; a circular device capable of rotating on its axis
