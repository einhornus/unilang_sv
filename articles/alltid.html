alltid (145)


Etymology

From all + tid, literally all time. Compare Danish altid, Dutch altijd, Icelandic ætíð, Norwegian Bokmål alltid, Norwegian Nynorsk alltid.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -iːd

Adverb

alltid (not comparable)

  1. always
    Sigge har alltid varit lång.
    Sigge has always been tall.
