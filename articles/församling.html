församling (8963)


Etymology

From Old Swedish forsambling, equivalent to församla + -ing. Cognate with Middle Low German vorsamlinge, vorsamelinge, Danish forsamling, Norwegian Bokmål forsamling, Norwegian Nynorsk forsamling, German Versammlung, Dutch verzameling and Afrikaans versameling.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

församling c

  1. an assembly, assemblage
  2. a parish
  3. a congregation
