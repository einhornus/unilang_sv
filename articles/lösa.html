Etymology 1

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

lösa

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of lös.

Etymology 2

From Old Swedish lø̄sa, from Old Norse leysa, from Proto-Germanic *lausijaną.

Verb

lösa (present löser, preterite löste, supine löst, imperative lös)

  1. to solve (a problem)
  2. to dissolve; to make something enter a solution
    Lös två teskedar salt i vattnet ― Dissolve two teaspoons of salt in the water
