uppgörelse (6623)


Etymology

göra upp + -else

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

uppgörelse c

  1. an agreement, a contract, a settlement
    uppgörelsen om Grekland stärker euron
    the settlement regarding Greece strengthens the euro
  2. a confrontation
    en uppgörelse med tanken om kulturell renhet
    a confrontation with the idea of cultural purity
