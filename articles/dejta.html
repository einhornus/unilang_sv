dejta (6977)


Etymology

From English date + -a or from dejt + -a.

Pronunciation

Verb

dejta (present dejtar, preterite dejtade, supine dejtat, imperative dejta)

  1. (intransitive, informal) to date
    Jag har dejtat ett tag nu.
    I've been dating for a while now.
  2. (transitive, informal) to date (someone)
    Jag har börjat dejta Karin.
    I've started to date Karin.
  3. (reciprocal, informal) to date each other
    Jag och Karin har börjat dejta.
    Karin and I have started dating.
