världskrig (20851)


Etymology

From värld + -s- + krig.

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -iːɡ

Noun

världskrig n

  1. a world war (a war involving the major nations of the world), the Great War
    Det var med underliga känslor man den 22 april 1917 mindes att det var tusen dagar sedan världskriget börjat.
    It was with strange feelings that one, on April 22, 1917, remembered that a thousand days had passed since the Great War had begun.
