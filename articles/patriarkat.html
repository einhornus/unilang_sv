patriarkat (119889)


Etymology

From French patriarcat, from Medieval Latin patriarchatus, used since 1610.

Noun

patriarkat n

  1. a patriarchy, a social system dominated by men
  2. a patriarchate, the rule or office of a patriarch, a high bishop
