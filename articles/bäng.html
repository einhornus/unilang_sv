bäng (86256)


Etymology

From Tavringer Romani beng, bäng (“devil, satan”), from Romani beng (“devil”). Related to Sanskrit व्यङ्ग (vyaṅga, “speckled, deformed”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bɛŋ/
  • Rhymes: -ɛŋ

Adjective

bäng (comparative mer bäng, superlative mest bäng)

  1. (slang) crazy, stupid
