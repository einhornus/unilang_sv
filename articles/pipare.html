pipare (124196)


Etymology

Related to pip (“to squeak, chirp”); compare the Danish pibe (“whistle, squeak”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpiːpaɾɛ/
  • Rhymes: -ɾɛ

Noun

pipare c
