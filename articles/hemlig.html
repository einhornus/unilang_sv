hemlig (2720)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hemeliker, from Old Norse heimiligr, from Middle Low German heimelik, from Proto-Germanic *haimalīkaz, from Proto-Germanic *haimaz (“home”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“to lie, settle”). Cognate with German heimlich, English homely.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

hemlig (comparative hemligare, superlative hemligast)

  1. secret (being or kept hidden)
