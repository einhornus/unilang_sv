anslagstavla (51592)


Etymology

From anslag + -s- + tavla.

Noun

anslagstavla c

  1. a noticeboard
    elektronisk anslagstavla
    bulletin board system

Usage notes

  • "Anslagstavlan" was a regular posting of public announcements on Swedish television in the 1970s and 1980s.
