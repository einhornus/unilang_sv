söta (2017)


Etymology

The verb is based on the adjective: söt + -a

Adjective

söta

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of söt.

Verb

söta (present sötar, preterite sötade, supine sötat, imperative söta)

  1. to sweeten; to make sweet to the taste
