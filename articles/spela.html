spela (387)


Etymology

From Old Norse spila, borrowed from Middle Low German spillen, from Proto-West Germanic *spilōn. Equivalent to spel + -a.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /speːla/
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

spela (present spelar, preterite spelade, supine spelat, imperative spela)

  1. to play; a game, a tune, a melody, an instrument or a role
  2. to gamble
  3. to wind (ropes and such)
