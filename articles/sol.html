sol (4084)


Etymology

From Old Swedish sōl, from Old Norse sól, from Proto-Germanic *sōwulō, from Proto-Indo-European *sóh₂wl̥.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /suːl/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

sol c

  1. sun
  2. (by extension) a star, especially when one considers things in its surroundings.
