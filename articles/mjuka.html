mjuka (5678)


Adjective

mjuka

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of mjuk.

Verb

mjuka (present mjukar, preterite mjukade, supine mjukat, imperative mjuka)

  1. to soften, to make soft
  2. (sports) to warm up

Usage notes

Virtually only used in the phrasal verb mjuka upp.
