regna (6015)


Etymology

Old Norse regna, from Proto-Germanic *regnōną, verbal from of *regną (“rain”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈreŋˌna/

Verb

regna (present regnar, preterite regnade, supine regnat, imperative regna)

  1. (impersonal) to rain
    Regnar det?
    Is it raining?
