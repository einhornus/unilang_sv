inrikespolitik (93688)


Etymology

inrikes + politik

Noun

inrikespolitik c

  1. domestic policy

Usage notes

  • This term can refer to anything domestic (taxes, roads, crime, schools), whereas an inrikesdepartement (“ministry of the interior”) typically deals with police matters. Thus, there is no direct connection between inrikespolitik and inrikesdepartement, as there is between utrikespolitik and utrikesdepartement.
