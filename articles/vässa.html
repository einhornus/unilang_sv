vässa (26443)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hvætia. Also see vass (“sharp”).

Verb

vässa (present vässar, preterite vässade, supine vässat, imperative vässa)

  1. to sharpen; to make sharp (as in, to be able to cut or pierce easily; or about an argument: to be able to convince easily)
