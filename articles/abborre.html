abborre (32334)


Etymology

From Old Swedish aghborre, aborre, from a combination of Proto-Indo-European *h₂eḱ- (“sharp, pointed”) (whence also Proto-Germanic *agjō, Swedish egg) and Proto-Germanic *burzô (whence Swedish borre), which is related to Swedish borste and English burr. Cognate with Danish aborre, Norwegian Bokmål abbor, Norwegian Nynorsk åbor, Faroese áburra and Icelandic aborri.

Noun

abborre c

  1. European perch, redfin perch, English perch; a fish of the family Percidae
