homosex (115942)


Etymology

Derived from homo (“homo(sexual)”) + sex (“sex”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈhuːmʊˌseks/

Noun

homosex n

  1. Homosexual sex.
    Parlamentet röstade för att göra homosex lagligt.
    "The parliament voted to make gay sex legal."

Usage notes

The term can be used neutrally, but is mostly used in contexts where homosexuality is described as problematic or controversial.
