avlagd (107178)


Etymology

past participle of avlägga.

Adjective

avlagd

  1. completed (of examinations, graduations that mark the completion of an education)
    Född i Arnäs i Ångermanland kom han efter avlagd lärarexamen 1879 till Hässjö
    Born in Arnäs in Ångermanland, he came, after having completed the teachers' seminar, in 1879 to Hässjö [to work as a teacher]
  2. old, no longer used (of clothes, that children grow out of)
    Anna har nu Stinas avlagda kjol
    Anna now wears Stina's old skirt
