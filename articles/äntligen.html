äntligen (968)


Etymology

From Middle Low German endeliken, from ende, equivalent to ändlig + -en. Cognate with Danish endelig, Norwegian Bokmål endelig, Norwegian Nynorsk endeleg, German endlich, Dutch eindelijk and English endly.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɛntˌlɪ(ɡ)ɛn/, (colloquial) /ˈɛŋkˌlɪ(ɡ)ɛn/
  • Compare the colloquial pronunciation, where -änt becomes -enk, with egentligen and ordentlig.
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

äntligen

  1. at last, finally

Interjection

äntligen!

  1. finally! (Used to express sudden pleasure, joy, or great excitement in regard to an announcement or a message.)
