samt (4204)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /samt/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Conjunction

samt

  1. and, and also, plus, as well as
    ko och tjur, höna och tupp samt tacka och bagge
    cow and bull, hen and cock, plus ewe and ram

Usage notes

  • In a list of pairs, samt is the more (och the less) important conjunction.
