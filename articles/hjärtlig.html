hjärtlig (42175)


Etymology

From hjärta + -lig. Compare similar construcuions with Danish hjertelig, Norwegian Bokmål hjertelig, Norwegian Nynorsk hjarteleg, German herzlich, Dutch hartelijk and English heartly.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²jæʈlɪ(ɡ)/

Adjective

hjärtlig (comparative hjärtligare, superlative hjärtligast)

  1. cordial, hearty
