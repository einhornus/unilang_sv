icke (2688)


Etymology

From Old Swedish ækki, ække, ikke. Originally the nominative neuter of Old Swedish ængin.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

icke (not comparable)

  1. (slightly formal) not, negating the verb in a sentence

Synonyms

  • ej, inte

Noun

icke n

  1. (logic) the Boolean function "not"
