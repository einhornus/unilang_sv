affär (1633)


Etymology

From French affaire.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -æːr

Noun

affär c

  1. a shop, a store
  2. a deal; an agreement between two parties to trade
  3. an affair; that which is done or is to be done; especially something which receives great public attention
  4. an affair; an adulterous relationship
