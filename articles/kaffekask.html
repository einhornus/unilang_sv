kaffekask (131232)


Etymology

From kaffe (“coffee”) + kask.

Noun

kaffekask c

  1. A coffee drink laced with alcohol. Traditionally made by placing a coin in the bottom of a cup and then filling the cup with coffee until the coin is not visible. A flavored schnapps is then added until the coin becomes visible again. This process is repeated until the cup is full.
    Synonym: kaffegök (disputed)
