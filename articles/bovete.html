bovete (97129)


Etymology

Derived from Middle Low German bokwete, equivalent to bok (“beech”) + vete (“wheat”). Related to Danish boghvede and possibly borrowed or calqued from it.

Noun

bovete n

  1. plant or seed of buckwheat, (Fagopyrum esculentum)
