åra (33167)


Etymology

From Old Norse ár, from Proto-Germanic *airō (“oar”). Cognate with Finnish airo.

Noun

åra c

  1. oar, paddle
    Vem kan segla förutan vind, vem kan ro utan åror?
    Who can sail without a wind, who can row without oars?
    (folk song lyrics)
