geting (21982)


Etymology

From Old Swedish gētunger, equivalent of get (“goat”) + -ing, from the antennae which resemble the horns of the goat. Compare Icelandic geitungur.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈjeːˌtɪŋ/
