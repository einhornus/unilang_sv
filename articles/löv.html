löv (9996)


Etymology

From Old Norse lauf, from Old Swedish løf, from Proto-Germanic *laubą, perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (“peel, break off”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -øːv

Noun

löv n

  1. (botany) leaf; an organ of a plant
  2. (computing, graph theory) leaf
