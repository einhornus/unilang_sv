ungersk (42267)


Adjective

ungersk (comparative ungerskare, superlative ungerskast)

  1. Hungarian; of or pertaining to present-day Hungary or the ethnic Hungarian people
  2. Hungarian; of or pertaining to the Kingdom of Hungary, during the days of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, regardless of ethnicity
