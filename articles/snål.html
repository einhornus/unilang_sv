snål (12250)


Etymology

From Old Swedish snāl (“cheap, greedy”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /snoːl/
  • Rhymes: -oːl

Adjective

snål (comparative snålare, superlative snålast)

  1. unwilling to give money; cheap, stingy
  2. meagre; deficient or inferior in amount, quality or extent
    1. (in compounds) containing a small amount or using few resources
  3. (of a wind) biting, cutting
