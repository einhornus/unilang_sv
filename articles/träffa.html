träffa (273)


Etymology

Borrowed from German treffen, from Proto-Germanic *drepaną.

Pronunciation

Verb

träffa (present träffar, preterite träffade, supine träffat, imperative träffa)

  1. to hit a target
  2. to meet
  3. to enter an agreement
