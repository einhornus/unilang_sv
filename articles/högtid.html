högtid (27147)


Etymology

From Old Swedish höghtiþ with the same meaning (alternatively "wedding"), from Low German hochtid (“important religious or secular festivity”). Compare German Hochzeit (“wedding”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈhœkːˌtiːd/, /ˈhøːɡˌtiːd/

Noun

högtid c

  1. A holiday, festival or similar festive occasion.
    Julen är min familjs viktigaste högtid.
    "Christmas is the most important holiday for my family."
