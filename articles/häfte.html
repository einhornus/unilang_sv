häfte (63192)


Etymology

Related to häfta (“to stick, to bind, to staple”), like German Heft

Noun

häfte (n)

  1. any papers that are stapled or bound together, a pamphlet, a booklet, a notebook
  2. a fascicle (a portion of a book, published separately), an issue of a journal or magazine (where the volume is the full year)
  3. a book (with soft covers)
