stjärt (14725)


Etymology

From Old Swedish stiærter, stærter, from Old Norse stertr, from Proto-Germanic *stertaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɧɛrt/
  • Rhymes: -ɛrt

Noun

stjärt c

  1. (somewhat colloquial) a bottom, a behind
  2. a tail of certain animals, primarily birds, fish, insects and reptiles
