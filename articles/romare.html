romare (7699)


Etymology

From Old Swedish romare, equivalent to Rom + -are.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²rʊˌmarɛ/

Noun

romare c

  1. (historical) A Roman, a native or resident of the Roman Empire.
  2. (rare) person from present-day Rome
