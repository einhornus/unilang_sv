folköl (70744)


Etymology

Compound of folk (“people”) + öl (“beer”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²fɔlkˌøːl/

Noun

folköl n or c

  1. a beer with an alcoholic content between 2.25% and 3.5%
  2. a serving, such as a bottle or a can, of such beer
