adoptera (9211)


Verb

adoptera (present adopterar, preterite adopterade, supine adopterat, imperative adoptera)

  1. to adopt; to take by choice into relationship, as, child, heir, friend, citizen, etc.; especially to take voluntarily (a child of other parents) to be in the place of, or as, one's own child.
