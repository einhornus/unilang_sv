funktion (6490)


Etymology

From Latin fūnctiō.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fɵŋkˈɧuːn/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

funktion c

  1. function; what something does or is used for
  2. (mathematics) function; a many-to-one relation; a relation in which each element of the domain is associated with exactly one element of the codomain.
  3. (computing) function; a routine that returns a result.
  4. (biology) function; the physiological activity of an organ or body part.
