gören (117931)


Verb

gören

  1. Obsolete plural form of gör, present tense of göra. 2nd person only
  2. Obsolete plural form of gör, imperative of göra. 2nd person only
    Och om I gören gott mot dem som göra eder gott, vad tack kunnen I få därför?
    And if ye do good to them which do good to you, what thank have ye? (Luke 6:33)
