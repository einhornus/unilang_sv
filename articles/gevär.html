gevär (2012)


Etymology

Either from German Gewehr, from Middle High German gewer, from Old High German giwerida or from Low German Gewehr. Cognate with Norwegian Bokmål gevær, Danish gevær and Dutch geweer. Related to värja (“to defend, to protect”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jɛˈvɛːr/, [ʝɛ̠ˈvæːɾ]
