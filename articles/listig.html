listig (13337)


Adjective

listig (comparative listigare, superlative listigast)

  1. cunning, sly
    Hunden kan icke vara listig som en räf, ej heller räfven trogen som en hund.
    The dog can not be sly as a fox, neither [can] the fox [be] faithful as a dog.
