brådis (33194)


Etymology

bråd + -is

Adverb

brådis (comparative mer brådis, superlative mest brådis)

  1. (colloquial) Synonym of bråttom.
    Jag har lite brådis så jag hinner inte snacka nå' mer.
    I'm in quite a hurry, so I don't have time to talk anymore.

Usage notes

Note that is Swedish you have hurry, whereas in English you are in a hurry.
