nog (130)


Etymology

From Old Norse nóg, nógr, gnógr, from Proto-Germanic *ganōgaz.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

nog (not comparable)

  1. enough, sufficient
    Har vi nog med mat för picknicken?
    Do we have enough food for the picnic?
  2. probably
    Det har vi nog.
    We probably do (have that).
