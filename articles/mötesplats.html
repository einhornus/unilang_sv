mötesplats (18243)


Etymology

Compound of möte (“meeting”) + -s- + plats (“place, point”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²møːtɛsˌplats/

Noun

mötesplats c

  1. place where a scheduled meeting takes place; rendezvous, tryst, venue
  2. passing place
