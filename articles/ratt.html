ratt (19674)


Etymology

From Middle Low German rat (“wheel”), from Old Saxon rath.

Noun

ratt c

  1. a steering wheel
  2. a knob (e.g. for volume control or tuning of a radio) - compare knapp
  3. (slang) a woman's breast
