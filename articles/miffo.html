miffo (21782)


Etymology

Clipping of missfoster (“miscarriage, freak”), with the -ss- removed because of pronunciation difficulties and an f added since it's a short vowel before it, + -o

Noun

miffo n

  1. (colloquial) an idiot, a freak, a weird, stupid person (strongly derogatory)
