jugge (79216)


Etymology

Clipping of jugoslav + -e, probably in analogy with finne (“Finn”), skotte (“Scot”), gute (“Gotlander”) etc.

Noun

jugge c

  1. (colloquial, sometimes derogatory) A person from a country that was a part of Yugoslavia.
