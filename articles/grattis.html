grattis (1014)


Etymology

Attested from 1934. Clipping of gratulerar + -is.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /¹ɡratɪs/

Interjection

grattis

  1. (informal) congratulations; congrats
    Grattis till segern.
    Congratulations on your victory.
    Grattis på födelsedagen!
    Happy birthday!
    Synonym: gratulerar (formal)
