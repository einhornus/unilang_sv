blekt (29644)


Etymology

past participle of bleka.

Adjective

blekt

  1. bleached (having been made pale, treated with bleach)
    en blekt tröja, den blekta tröjan
    a bleached sweater, the bleached sweater
  2. absolute indefinite neuter form of blek.
    ett blekt barn, det bleka barnet
    a pale child, the pale child
