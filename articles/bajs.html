bajs (8144)


Etymology

Attested since 1842 as baj ("feces"), usually in the expression fy baj, a form of reprimand used for and by small children. Johan Ernst Rietz Svenskt dialektlexikon ("Swedish dialect dictionary") also suggests[1] a relation to Middle High German baht.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bajs/

Noun

bajs n

  1. (colloquial, childish) Feces, poop.
    Du luktar bajs.
    You smell [like] poop.
