Etymology 1

From Old Swedish kiærne, from Old Norse kjarni, from Proto-Germanic *kernô (“core, kernel”). Cognate with Icelandic kjarni, German Kern, Dutch kern, Luxembourgish Kär.

Noun

kärna c

  1. a seed, a pip
  2. a kernel, a nucleus, a core
  3. a pit (of a cherry)
