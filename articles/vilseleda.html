vilseleda (22291)


Etymology

vilse + leda

Verb

vilseleda (present vilseleder, preterite vilseledde, supine vilselett, imperative vilseled)

  1. to lead astray, to consciously point someone in the wrong direction (in a concrete sense)
  2. to deceive, to mislead (in a more abstracte sense)
