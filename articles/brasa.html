brasa (11102)


Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brasō (“gleed, crackling coal”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewh₁- (“to seethe, boil, brew”), or from *bʰres- (“to crack, break, burst”).[1]

Noun

brasa c

  1. a small, controlled fire used for warmth
