fisa (20668)


Etymology

From Old Norse físa (“to break wind”), from Proto-Germanic *fīsaną (“to break wind”).

Verb

fisa (present fiser, preterite fes, supine fisit, imperative fis)

  1. to fart (to emit flatulent gases)
