femti (53043)


Etymology

Borrowed from Norwegian femti and Swedish femtio, from Old Norse fimtigi.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [ˈfɛmˌtˢi]

Numeral

femti

  1. fifty
    Synonym: halvtreds

Usage notes

  • Sometimes used for clarity on checks and documents and in communication with other Scandinavians.
