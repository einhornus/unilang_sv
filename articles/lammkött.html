lammkött (35515)


Etymology

From Old Swedish lamba kiøt, equivalent to lamm (“lamb”) + kött (“meat, flesh”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlamːˌɕœtː/

Noun

lammkött n

  1. mutton
    Lammköttet är slut i alla butiker.
    "All the stores are out of mutton."
  2. (slang, derogatory) A young person, usually female, as an object of sexual desire; nymphet.
    Det var massor med läckert lammkött på festen igår.
    "There was plenty of scrumptious nymphets at the party yesterday."
