toffelhjälte (98535)


Etymology

Compound of toffel (“slipper”) + hjälte (“hero”). From the expression stå under toffeln (“to be completely subordinate to one’s wife”, literally “to stand under the slipper”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²tɔfɛlˌjɛltɛ/

Noun

toffelhjälte c

  1. a husband who plays a subordinate role in a marriage; an uxorious husband; a henpecked man
