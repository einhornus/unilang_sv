tigga (9047)


Etymology

From Old Swedish þiggia, from Old Norse þiggja (“to receive”), from Proto-Germanic *þigjaną. Cognates: see there.

Verb

tigga (present tigger, preterite tiggde, supine tiggt, imperative tigg)

  1. beg; to request the help of someone
  2. beg; to plead with someone for help or for a favor
