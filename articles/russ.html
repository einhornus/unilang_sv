russ (5567)


Etymology

From Old Swedish russ, from Old Norse hross, from Proto-Germanic *hrussą, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱers-.

Noun

russ n

  1. (dialect) a horse
  2. a horse of the race Gotland pony / gotlandsruss
