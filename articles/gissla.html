gissla (88853)


Etymology

gissel (“whip, staff”) + -a

Verb

gissla (present gisslar, preterite gisslade, supine gisslat, imperative gissla)

  1. to whip, to flagellate
  2. to torture, to hurt
  3. to joke about (someone), to parody
