lie (39401)


Etymology

From Old Swedish līe, lē, from Old Norse lé, from Proto-Germanic *lewô, from Proto-Indo-European *leu- (“to cut”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /liːɛ/

Noun

lie c

  1. scythe; an instrument for mowing grass, grain, or the like.
