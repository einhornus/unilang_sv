sälja (932)


Etymology

From Old Swedish sælia, from Old Norse selja (“sell”), from Proto-Germanic *saljaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɛːlˌja/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: säl‧ja

Verb

sälja (present säljer, preterite sålde, supine sålt, imperative sälj)

  1. to sell
    Har ni sålt huset?
    Have you sold the house?
    Karin säljer bilar.
    Karin sells cars.
