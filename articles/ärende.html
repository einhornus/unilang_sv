ärende (4523)


Etymology

From Old Swedish ærande, ærinde, ærende, from Old Norse erendi, ørendi, eyrendi (“errand”), from Proto-Germanic *airundiją. Cognate with Danish ærinde, Norwegian ærend, dialectal German Erend and English errand.

Pronunciation

Noun

ärende n
