anmäla (3614)


Etymology

From an- + mäla (“speak, utter”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

anmäla (present anmäler, preterite anmälde, supine anmält, imperative anmäl)

  1. report, notify authorities about something
    anmäla (någon, något) till polisen
    report (someone, something) to the police
  2. sign up (for an activity)
    Vilken kurs har du anmält dig till?
    Which course have you signed up for?
  3. to make known that one has arrived
    När du kommer fram till konferensen, måste du anmäla dig i receptionen.
    When you arrive to the conference venue, you have to report to the reception.
