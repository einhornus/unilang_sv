pung (10226)


Etymology

From Old Swedish punger, from Old Norse pungr, itself of unknown origin.

Noun

pung c

  1. purse (small bag used to keep small items)
  2. scrotum
    Synonym: scrotum
  3. a pouch in marsupials where it rears its young during their early infancy
