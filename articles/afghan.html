afghan (64529)


Etymology

From Persian افغان (afğân, “Afghan”) via Prakrit, from Sanskrit अवगाण (avagāṇa, “Afghan”).

Noun

afghan c

  1. Afghan; person, chiefly male, from Afghanistan
  2. Synonym of afghanhund (“Afghan Hound”).
