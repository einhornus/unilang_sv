patti (22156)


Etymology

From Swedish spatt.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈpɑtːi/, [ˈpɑt̪ːi]
  • Rhymes: -ɑtːi
  • Syllabification: pat‧ti

Noun

patti

  1. A firm, smooth swelling, a lump.
    Käsivarressani on outo patti.
    There's a strange lump in my arm.
  2. (chess) stalemate
