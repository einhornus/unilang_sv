utskrift (24195)


Etymology

ut + skrift, related to skriva ut (“to print out”)

Noun

utskrift c

  1. (computing) printing (the activity), a print job
    vill du avbryta utskriften?
    do you want to cancel the print job?
  2. a printout (the printed paper)
    kan vi slänga de här utskrifterna?
    can we junk these printouts?
