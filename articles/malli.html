malli (42046)


Etymology

Borrowed from Swedish mall, probably itself borrowed from Dutch mal, from Old French mole, modle, ultimately from Latin modulus.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmɑlːi/, [ˈmɑlːi]
  • Rhymes: -ɑlːi
  • Syllabification: mal‧li

Noun

malli

  1. model, template
  2. model, mannequin (person who models clothes)
  3. example, a role model, a (good) influence
    Hän ei näytä lapsille hyvää mallia.
    He's not a good role model for kids.
