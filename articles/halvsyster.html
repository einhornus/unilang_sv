halvsyster (41154)


Etymology

From halv- + syster.

Noun

halvsyster c

  1. a half sister
    [Christina Reginas] far hette Elias Forsberg och var kryddkrämare. Elias Forsbergs son Johan Eliasson Forsberg gifte sig med Catharina Buchau, dotter till Jacob Buchau i hans första äktenskap med Anna Maria Depken. Det vill säga: Jacob Buchaus andra hustru, Christina Regina Forsberg[sic] var halvsyster med Jacob Buchaus svärson.
    [Christina Regina's] father was named Elias Forsberg and was a vendor of spices. Elias Forsberg's son Johan Eliasson Forsberg married Catharina Buchau, daughter of Jacob Buchau from his first marriage with Anna Maria Depken. That is: Jacob Bucha's second wife, Christina Regina Forsberg, was the half sister of Jacob Buchau's son-in-law [Johan Eliasson Forsberg].
