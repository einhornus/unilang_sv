jordgubbe (41020)


Etymology

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

jordgubbe c

  1. a strawberry, from Fragaria × ananassa
    Synonym: ananassmultron (obsolete, 18th century)
  2. (dialect) a wild strawberry, from Fragaria vesca
  3. (obsolete, 17th century) a musk strawberry, from Fragaria moschata
  4. (obsolete, 17th century) a creamy strawberry, from Fragaria viridis
