kyckling (2182)


Etymology

From Old Swedish kyklinger, from Old Norse kjúklingr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɕʏkˌlɪŋ/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kyckling c

  1. (countable) chick; young chicken
    Jag tycker kycklingar är söta.
    I think chicks are cute.
  2. (uncountable) chicken (the meat from this bird eaten as food)
    Serverar ni kyckling på denna restaurang?
    Do you serve chicken in this restaurant?
