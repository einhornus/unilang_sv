spår (1420)


Etymology

From Old Norse spor, from Proto-Germanic *spurą.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

spår n

  1. a track; a mark left by something that has passed along
  2. a track; a mark or impression left by the foot
  3. a rail track
  4. a track; sound stored on a record
