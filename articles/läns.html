läns (75812)


Adjective

läns

  1. empty, dry, free of water (of a boat, being emptied)
    pumpa läns
    pump (a boat) dry

Noun

läns

  1. (uncountable) a following wind, running downwind, running before the wind, sailing with the wind from straight behind
  2. a boom, a floating barrier for oil spill containment
  3. indefinite genitive singular of län
  4. indefinite genitive plural of län
