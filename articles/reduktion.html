reduktion (107942)


Etymology

From Latin reductio, used in Swedish since 1660.

Noun

reduktion c

  1. a reduction, a transfer back to a previous state or location; a decrease
  2. a reduction, a chemical reaction
  3. a confiscation of (previously granted) lands from the nobility to the crown (state)
