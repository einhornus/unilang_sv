rör (344)


Etymology

From Old Swedish rør, from Old Norse reyrr.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

rör n

  1. a reed, a cane, a straw (of some tall grasses, including sugar canes, rattan and bamboo)
    italienskt rör
    Arundo donax
    spanskt rör
    rattan
  2. a cane (used for walking)
  3. (music) a reed (on some woodwind instruments: clarinet, saxophone, oboe)
  4. a pipe (hollow tube), a tube
  5. a skate
  6. (electronics) a vacuum tube
