luffare (9394)


Etymology

luffa + -are

Noun

luffare c

  1. a vagabond, a tramp, bum
    Synonym: vagabond
  2. (in compounds) traveller with several journeys on the same type of transportation.
    tågluffare ― person who travels long distances by hopping off and on trains
    båtluffare ― person who travels from island to island with different boats in an archipelago
