bibliotek (7827)


Etymology

Borrowed from French bibliothèque, from Latin bibliothēca (“library”), originally from Ancient Greek βιβλιοθήκη (bibliothḗkē, “book-room”), from βιβλίον (biblíon, “book”) +‎ θήκη (thḗkē, “box, chest”).
