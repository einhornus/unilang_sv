akta (895)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

akta (present aktar, preterite aktade, supine aktat, imperative akta)

  1. to take care of, to be careful with
  2. to pay attention to, to mind
    Akta huvudet!
    Mind your head!
