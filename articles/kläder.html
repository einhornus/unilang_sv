kläder (832)


Etymology

From Old Swedish klædhe, from Old Norse klæði. Compare kläde and English cloth.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈklɛːdɛr/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

kläder c pl

  1. clothes, garments, items of clothing

Usage notes

For a single garment, (ett) klädesplagg is used.

Verb

kläder

  1. present tense of kläda.
