Etymology 1

Adjective

täcka

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of täck.

Etymology 2

From Old Swedish þækkia, from Old Norse þekja, from Proto-Germanic *þakjaną; related to English thatch.[1]

Verb

täcka (present täcker, preterite täckte, supine täckt, imperative täck)

  1. to cover; to place something over
  2. to cover; to provide enough money for
  3. to cover; to protect someone with the help of force or the threat of force
