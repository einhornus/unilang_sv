vårdcentral (60712)


Etymology

vård + central

Noun

vårdcentral c

  1. a district health centre, a general practice clinic
    Alla vårdcentraler i länet har öppet dagtid varje vardag.
    All district health centres are open during the day on weekdays.

Usage notes

  • The district health centres are also referred to as primärvård (“primary care”), where hospitals are the secondary level
