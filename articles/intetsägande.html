intetsägande (59054)


Etymology

intet (“nothing”) + sägande (“saying”). Compare Danish intetsigende, Norwegian Bokmål intetsigende, Norwegian Nynorsk inkjeseiande, German nichtssagend, Dutch nietszeggend and Afrikaans niksseggend, niksseggende.

Adjective

intetsägande (comparative mer intetsägande, superlative mest intetsägande)

  1. inane, bland
    Rapporten om klottret var lång men intetsägande.
    The rapport of the graffiti was long but inane.
