krycka (36752)


Etymology

From Old Swedish krykkia, from Middle Low German krucke, krocke, krücke, from Old Saxon krukka, from Proto-Germanic *krukjō (whence also English crutch), from Proto-Indo-European *grewg-.

Noun

krycka c

  1. crutch; a device to assist in motion
