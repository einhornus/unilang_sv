ligga (1111)


Etymology

From Old Swedish liggia, from Old Norse liggja, from Proto-Germanic *ligjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *legʰ-.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

ligga (present ligger, preterite låg, supine legat, imperative ligg)

  1. lie; be in a horizontal position
  2. lie; to be placed
    Boken ligger på bordet ― The book is lying on the table
  3. (slang) to have sex
