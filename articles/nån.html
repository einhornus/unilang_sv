nån (121)


Etymology

Syncopic form of någon.

Pronoun

nån c

  1. (colloquial) Pronunciation spelling of någon (“someone”).
    kära nån ― oh, dear!
    Kan nån hjälpa mig? ― Can someone help me?

Determiner

nån c (neuter nåt, plural nåra)

  1. (colloquial) Pronunciation spelling of någon (“some, any”)
    Undra om det nån jacka so passar mig?
    I wonder if there's any jacket that fits me.
