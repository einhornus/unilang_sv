bokhandel (19417)


Etymology

Compound of bok + handel.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²buːkˌhandɛl/

Noun

bokhandel c

  1. (countable) bookstore (a store that sells books)
  2. (uncountable) the act of selling books

Usage notes

  • Sometimes, a suppletive plural form boklådor (plural of the near-synonymous boklåda (“bookshop”)) is found. This was previously a recommended plural form, as the normal plural form bokhandlarna can also be the pluralform of bokhandlare (“bookseller”). This recommendation is no longer upheld.
