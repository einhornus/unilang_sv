ganska (503)


Etymology

From Middle Low German gans, gansch, from Middle High German ganz, from Old High German ganz, from Proto-West Germanic *gant.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

ganska (not comparable)

  1. pretty, quite, rather
    Den här melonen är ganska smaklös.
    This melon is rather tasteless.
    Min bror är ganska lång.
    My brother is pretty tall.

Synonyms

  • rätt så
  • tämligen
