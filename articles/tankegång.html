tankegång (44256)


Etymology

Compound of Swedish tanke, from Old Norse þanki (“thought”), and Swedish gång, from Old Swedish ganger (“act of walking, movement forward, path”), via Old Norse gangr, from Proto-Germanic *gangaz; similar to German Gedankengang.

Noun

tankegång c

  1. reasoning, train of thought, thought process, way of thinking
