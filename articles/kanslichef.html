kanslichef (120256)


Etymology

kansli + chef

Noun

kanslichef c

  1. a head of administration[1] (at an embassy)
  2. a senior judge referee[1] (Högsta domstolen, Regeringsrätten)
  3. a secretary[1] (in a utskott (“parliamentary committee”))
  4. a chief executive[1] (in a kommun (“municipality”) and landsting (“county council”))
  5. an administrative director[1] (other offices)
