kupa (23725)


Noun

kupa c

  1. a hood, cover
  2. a cup (of a bra)
  3. (short for “bikupa”) a beehive

Verb

kupa (present kupar, preterite kupade, supine kupat, imperative kupa)

  1. to fold one’s hand as into a cup
  2. to earth up; to cover the stem of a plant with soil
