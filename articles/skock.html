skock (25807)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈskɔk/

Noun

skock c

  1. A large, unspecified number of something (compare the plural use of English dozen).
  2. An old unitless measure indicating sixty (compare the archaic German Schock in the sense of sixty).
