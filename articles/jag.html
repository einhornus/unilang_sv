jag (1)


Etymology

From Old Swedish iak, jæk, from Old Norse jak (compare Old West Norse ek), from Proto-Norse ᛖᚲ (ek), from Proto-Germanic *ek, from Proto-Indo-European *éǵh₂.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈjɑː(ɡ)/
  • audio
    		(file)

Pronoun

jag

  1. I
    Jag läser en bok.
    I'm reading a book.
    Bara du och jag.
    Just you and me.
