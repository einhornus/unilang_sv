vatten (514)


Etymology

From Old Norse vatn, from Proto-Germanic *watōr, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wódr̥.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈvatːɛn/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

vatten n

  1. water (uncountable)
  2. a body of water (countable)
