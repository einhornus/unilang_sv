pricka (13457)


Etymology

prick + -a

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

pricka (present prickar, preterite prickade, supine prickat, imperative pricka)

  1. to hit (the right spot)
  2. to give (someone) a remark or warning
  3. to put one or more seamark(s) in place (applies to such particular seamarks as described by Sw. prick)
    Att pricka (ut) en farled
    To mark a safe water route
    Att pricka (ut) ett grund
    To mark a shallow
