Pronunciation

Verb

knäcka (present knäcker, preterite knäckte, supine knäckt, imperative knäck)

  1. to crack, to break, to snap
  2. to crack (a secret code, an encryption)
  3. (colloquial) to work (especially shorter jobs for hire)
