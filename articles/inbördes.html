inbördes (33223)


Etymology

From in + bord, from Old Swedish inbyrþis. Compare Old English innanbordes (“at home, onboard”)

Adjective

inbördes

  1. (archaic) indoors, domestic, pertaining to the home and to the own country; still in the compound inbördeskrig
  2. mutual

Synonyms

  • ömsesidig

Adverb

inbördes

  1. (archaic) indoors, domestically
  2. mutually

Synonyms

  • ömsesidigt

Related terms

  • inbördeskrig
