Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈføːˌrʉːt/, /fœˈrʉːt/
  • Rhymes: -ʉːt

Adverb

förut (not comparable)

  1. before (at an earlier time)
    Jag har aldrig gjort detta förut.
    I've never done this before.
  2. previously, earlier

Etymology 2

för + ut, from the noun för (“bow of a ship”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈføːrʉːt/

Adverb

förut

  1. (nautical) bowwards; towards the bow
