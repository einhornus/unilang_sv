högst (2507)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

högst

  1. superlative predicative form of hög.

Adverb

högst

  1. superlative degree of högt: most; highly, to a very high degree
    En högst tveksam idé.
    A most doubtful idea.
  2. at most, not more than
    Stömavbrottet kan högst ha varat fem minuter.
    The blackout cannot have lasted for more than five minutes.
