utom (1130)


Etymology

From ut + om

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ʉ̟ːtɔm/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Preposition

utom

  1. except for, but, beyond
    alla var där utom han
    everyone was there but him
  2. outside, out of
    utom synhåll
    out of sight

Usage notes

  • As opposed to most prepositions, utom often governs the subject form of pronouns as opposed to the object form:
alla utom jag
everyone but me (literally ’but I’)
