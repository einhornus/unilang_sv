konst (2493)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

konst c

  1. an art (a liberal art, to make artworks)
    Livet är kort, men konsten är evig.
    Life is short, but the art is forever.
  2. an art, a science, a trick, knowledge (something to learn and know)
    konsten att flyga
    the art of flight
  3. (dated) an art, an invention, an engine, a machinery, a pump (something artificial)
    Till detta ändamål använder man vanligtvis konster eller pumpverk, medelst hvilka vattnet pumpas upp ...
    For this purpose pumps are commonly used, with which the water is pumped up ...

Declension

Declension of konst
Singular Plural
Indefinite Definite Indefinite Definite
Nominative konst konsten konster konsterna
Genitive konsts konstens konsters konsternas

Usage notes

As labeled, the third definition isn't used anymore although it has led to a prefix konst-, meaning "artificial", which still is very frequently used.
