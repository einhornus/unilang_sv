säte (16355)


Etymology

From Old Norse sæti, from Proto-Germanic *sētiją.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

säte n

  1. seat
    1. a place to seat, especially in a vehicle
    2. the location of a governing body
    3. (rare) the part of an object or individual (usually the buttocks) directly involved in sitting
    4. (rare) the part of a piece of clothing (usually pants or trousers) covering the buttocks
