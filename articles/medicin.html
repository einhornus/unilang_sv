medicin (1730)


Etymology

From Latin medicīna.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /mɛdɪˈsiːn/
  • Audio

Noun

medicin c

  1. (uncountable) medicine (the art of curing illness)
  2. a medicine, a drug (substance which promotes healing)
