skälig (30977)


Etymology

From Old Swedish skiælīker, see skäl (“reason”) + -ig (“-y”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɧɛːlɪ(ɡ)/
  • Rhymes: -ɛːlɪɡ

Noun

skälig (comparative skäligare, superlative skäligast)

  1. fair, reasonable

Usage notes

Often used in an economic sense, as in skäligt pris (”reasonable price”), etc.
