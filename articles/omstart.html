omstart (30302)


Etymology

< Swedish omstart (“restart”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈomstɑrt/, [ˈo̞ms̠t̪ɑrt̪]
  • Rhymes: -omstɑrt
  • Syllabification: oms‧tart

Interjection

omstart

  1. A word that a spectator may shout during a spex (speksi) show to make the actors replay the previous scene.
