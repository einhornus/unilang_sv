lagman (67379)


Etymology

lag + man

Noun

lagman c

  1. a chief judge[1] of a tingsrätt (“district court”) and länsrätt (“county administrative court”)
  2. a president of a city court (in Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö)[1]
  3. a head of division at a court of appeal
