ögonblick (694)


Etymology

From ögon + blick. Compare Danish øjeblik, Dutch ogenblik, German Augenblick, Icelandic augnablik, Yiddish אויגנבליק (oygnblik), English eyeblink.

Pronunciation

  • audio

Noun

ögonblick n

  1. a moment, blink of an eye (a brief period of time)
    Vänta ett ögonblick
    Wait a moment
