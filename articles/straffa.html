straffa (5316)


Etymology

straff + -a

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

straffa (present straffar, preterite straffade, supine straffat, imperative straffa)

  1. to punish (to cause to suffer for crime or misconduct)
  2. (reflexive) to cause future setbacks, to be tempting (for the moment) but likely to cause trouble later
    Att festa till sent kan straffa sig på jobbet dagen därpå.
    Partying until late can cause trouble at work the next day.
