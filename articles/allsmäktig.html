allsmäktig (21305)


Etymology

From Old Saxon alomahtig, from Proto-Germanic *alamahtīgaz (“almighty”).

Adjective

allsmäktig (comparative allsmäktigare, superlative allsmäktigast)

  1. powerful
  2. omnipotent, almighty
