arkitektur (21252)


Etymology

arkitekt + -ur

Noun

arkitektur c

  1. architecture (art and science of designing buildings and other structures)
  2. an architecture; a specific school of thought on how to design and construct buildings and structures
    Det var ett fint exempel på neoklassisk arkitektur.
    It was a nice example of neoclassical architecture.
