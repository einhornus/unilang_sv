kompromissa (16642)


Etymology

kompromiss + -a

Verb

kompromissa (present kompromissar, preterite kompromissade, supine kompromissat, imperative kompromissa)

  1. to compromise; to make both parties satisfied by letting them both get something of what they want but not all
    Jag ville välja fem och han tio men vi kompromissade och kom överens med att vi får välja varannan gång.
    I wanted to choose five and he ten but we compromised and agreed to that we get to choose every other time.
