bedriva (25471)


Etymology

From Old Swedish bedriva, from Middle Low German bedrîven. Equivalent to be- + driva. Cognate with Danish bedrive, Norwegian Bokmål bedrive, German betreiben, Dutch bedrijven and English bedrive.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

bedriva (present bedriver, preterite bedrev, supine bedrivit, imperative bedriv)

  1. to operate, to run, to manage
