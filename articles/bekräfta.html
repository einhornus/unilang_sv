bekräfta (3536)


Etymology

From Middle Low German bekreften or German bekräften (rare, more often bekräftigen), from Middle High German bekreften. Equivalent of be- + kraft + -a. The synonym bekräftiga was stopped being used during the 17th centuary. Cognate with Danish bekræfte, Norwegian Bokmål bekrefte and Norwegian Nynorsk bekrefte, bekrefta.

Verb

bekräfta (present bekräftar, preterite bekräftade, supine bekräftat, imperative bekräfta)

  1. to confirm, to acknowledge
