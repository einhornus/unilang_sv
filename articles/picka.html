picka (40006)


Etymology

From Old Swedish pikka, from Old Norse, from Proto-Germanic *pikkōną. Compare English pick.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²pɪka/, [²pʰɪkːa]
  • Rhymes: -²ɪka

Verb

picka (present pickar, preterite pickade, supine pickat, imperative picka)

  1. To pick up with the beak (of a bird); to peck.
