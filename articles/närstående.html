närstående (25175)


Etymology

nära + stående

Adjective

närstående

  1. closely related

Noun

närstående c

  1. a close relative, a closely related person, a family member
    detta kan påverka den närståendes egen hälsa.
    this can impact the relative's own health.

Usage notes

  • The word is used in various Swedish laws and court sentences, with slightly differing definitions, as the Wikipedia article explains.
  • On its way from adjective to noun (en närstående person → en närstående), a proper definite form declension (närståenden) is very rare. It is often avoided as in the example sentence.
