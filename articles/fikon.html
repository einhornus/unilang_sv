fikon (23593)


Etymology

Borrowed Middle Low German vige, from Vulgar Latin *fīca, from Latin fīcus (“fig tree, fruit”), + -on (neutral noun-forming suffix; used in the names of fruits and berries).

Noun

fikon n

  1. a fig (a tree and its fruits)
