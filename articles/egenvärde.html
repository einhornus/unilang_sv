egenvärde (131577)


Etymology

egen + värde

Noun

egenvärde n

  1. (countable, linear algebra) eigenvalue (specific value related to a matrix)
  2. (uncountable) internal value; the value or usefulness of something in its own right, unrelated to its usefulness elsewhere
