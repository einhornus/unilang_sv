lärande (53229)


Etymology

present participle of lära

Adjective

lärande

  1. learning, teaching, which stimulates learning
    en lärande organisation
    a learning organization, one where the members learn

Usage notes

  • Teach and learn use the same verb in Swedish (lära ut, lära in), hence the double meaning of the present participle.
