växla (7268)


Etymology

From Proto-Germanic *wihslōną, equivalent to växel + -a.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

växla (present växlar, preterite växlade, supine växlat, imperative växla)

  1. to exchange, change, switch, swap (one thing for another)
  2. to change (money), give change
  3. to switch (a train towards its destination)
  4. to switch (a telephone call to its destination)
  5. to shift gears (in a car)
    motorn går på för lågt varv, du måste växla ner ― the engine is running too slow, you must shift down
  6. to put more (less) energy into an activity
    valkampanjen växlar upp ― the election campaign is shifting to a higher gear
