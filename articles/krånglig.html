krånglig (37710)


Adjective

krånglig (comparative krångligare, superlative krångligast)

  1. arduous, intricate, complicated; causing trouble and hassle; besvärlig (“troublesome”)
  2. (archaic) krokig (“crooked”)
  3. difficult, hard, tedious, tiresome, awkward, annoying
  4. cramped, confined, clunky, tangled; causing claustrophobia; trångt (“tight”)
  5. (of a machine, method, mechanic, organ) easily brought out of normal function; faulty, malfunctioning
  6. dealing with several things simultaneously; requires multitasking
  7. quarrelsome, inconsequential, incomprehensible, ambiguous
