inte (5)


Etymology

From Old Swedish ænkti, ænti, ingti, from a late form of the nominative neuter of Old Swedish ængin. For an earlier form, see icke.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɪntɛ/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

inte (not comparable)

  1. not
    Jag har inte gjort det.
    I have not done that.

Synonyms

  • ej, icke

Derived terms
