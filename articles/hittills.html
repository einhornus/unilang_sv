hittills (2594)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

hittills (not comparable)

  1. so far, heretofore, until now, until this point in time; yet
    Han har hittills aldrig kommit försent.
    He has never yet been late.
    Lagets nya tränare har hittills sett sitt lag vinna allt.
    The team's new trainer has so far seen her team win everything.

Usage notes

  • Used about conditions which have been true up to the present point in time.
  • Keep in mind the difference to dittills "until that point in time".

Synonyms

  • ännu

Related terms

  • dittills
