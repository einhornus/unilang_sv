diskotek (41334)


Etymology

The first sense (analogous to bibliotek) was used at least since 1954. The second sense arrived in the middle 1970s.

Noun

diskotek n

  1. (dated) a collection of gramophone records (disks)
  2. disco, discotheque (nightclub)
