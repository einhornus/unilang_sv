tillsammans (391)


Etymology

From Old Swedish til saman, til samans, from Middle Low German tosamene. Cognate with Danish tilsammen, Norwegian Bokmål til sammen, tilsammen, German zusammen, Saterland Frisian tousommen, Middle English tosamen, Dutch tezamen and Luxembourgish zesummen.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /tɪ(l)¹samans/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

tillsammans (not comparable)

  1. together
  2. in a relationship
    Vi är tillsammans.
    We're a couple.
    Han är tillsammans med henne.
    He's in relationship with her.
