stycke (6721)


Etymology

From Old Norse stykki, from Proto-Germanic *stukkiją, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewg-. Cognate with Old English stycce.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Noun

stycke n

  1. a piece (part of something, a part of a distance)
  2. a piece (a theatre play)
  3. a piece (a cannon)
  4. a piece (of music)
  5. a paragraph (of text)
