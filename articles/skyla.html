skyla (30968)


Etymology

From Old Norse skýla, from Proto-Germanic *skeulijaną, cognate with Danish skjule, Dutch schuilen. Derived from the noun *skeulą.

Verb

skyla (present skyler, preterite skylde, supine skylt, imperative skyl)

  1. cover; as to hide or conceal from direct view
