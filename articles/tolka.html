tolka (8972)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

tolka (present tolkar, preterite tolkade, supine tolkat, imperative tolka)

  1. to interpret; as of translating orally between languages (cf översätta)
  2. to interpret; to explain or tell the meaning of
  3. to interpret; to apprehend and represent by means of art
  4. to travel on bike or skis by being pulled by an animal (such as a horse, dog or reindeer) or a vehicle; to skijor
