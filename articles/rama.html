rama (10234)


Etymology

From ram (“frame”) + -a.

Verb

rama (present ramar, preterite ramade, supine ramat, imperative rama)

  1. to frame (put a frame around)
  2. (Scania, Malmö) to give somebody a lift on a bicycle
