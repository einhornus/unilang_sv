skepp (1381)


Etymology

From Old Norse skip, from Proto-Germanic *skipą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɧɛp/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

skepp n

  1. a ship (large water vessel)
  2. each third of the nave, a large section of the open space in a church as separated by columns
