ide (3450)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hiþ (“a beaver's den”). Cognate with English hide, possibly from a Germanic root hīwa-.

Noun

ide n

  1. a den for the hibernation of a bear or badger
    att gå i ide
    to den, to hibernate, to go into hiding
