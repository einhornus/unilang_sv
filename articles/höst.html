höst (8475)


Etymology

From Old Norse haust from earlier haustr (masculine), from Proto-Germanic *harbistaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kerp-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /hœst/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

höst c

  1. autumn (season)
  2. (figurative) the time towards the end of a life; the time of old age
