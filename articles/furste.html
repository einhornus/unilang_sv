furste (16446)


Etymology

From German Fürst.

Noun

furste c

  1. prince

Usage notes

The son of a king is called prins (“prince”). Furste is used for monarchs who are not titled king or emperor. Swedish translations of Machiavelli's work are titled Fursten.
