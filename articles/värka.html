värka (41595)


Etymology

värk + -a

Pronunciation

  • Homophone: verka

Verb

värka (present värker, preterite värkte, supine värkt, imperative värk)

  1. to ache; to be the source of a continued dull pain
  2. (värka fram, värka ut) to produce under great pain, to write despite writer's block or disabilities
    Som nämnts tidigare har budgeten värkts fram under kort tid.
    As mentioned before the budget was created in a short time.
