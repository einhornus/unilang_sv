när (52)


Etymology

From Old Norse nær, from Proto-Germanic *nēhwiz.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -æːr

Adverb

när (not comparable)

  1. when
    När börjar filmen?
    When does the movie begin?
  2. (about time) as
    När jag skulle gå hem,...
    As I was about to go home,...

Verb

när

  1. present tense of nära.
  2. imperative of nära.
