käka (6329)


Etymology

Most likely derived from käke (“jaw”) + -a.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɕɛˈka/

Verb

käka (present käkar, preterite käkade, supine käkat, imperative käka)

  1. (slang) to eat
  2. (dated) to chew on something chewy
