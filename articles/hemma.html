hemma (335)


Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: hämma

Adverb

hemma (not comparable)

  1. at home; at one’s place of residence
  2. at home; in the home of one’s parents
    hälsa dem där hemma
    bring (my) greetings to those back home

Usage notes

  • Meaning number 2 presupposes that one's parents still live where one grew up.
