sjuk (705)


Etymology

From Old Swedish siūker, from Old Norse sjúkr, from Proto-Germanic *seukaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɧʉːk/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ʉːk

Adjective

sjuk (comparative sjukare, superlative sjukast)

  1. sick, ill (having a disease)
  2. (colloquial) sick, disturbed, mentally unstable
  3. sick (in bad taste)
    Ett sjukt skämt.
    A sick joke.
