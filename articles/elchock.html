elchock (40200)


Etymology

el + chock

Noun

elchock c

  1. electroconvulsive therapy (used mostly in plural: elchocker)

Usage notes

  • electric shock (by accident) is translated with stöt, elstöt. The word elchock is only used for the therapeutic use.
