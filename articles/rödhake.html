rödhake (62025)


Etymology

Either: röd + hake, variant of hakel, related to English hackle. Same word found in mässhake.[1]

or

röd + hake, variant of haka (“chin”).[2]

Noun

rödhake c

  1. a robin (Erithacus rubecula)
