bilaga (41274)


Etymology

From the adjective bilagd (“attached”), past participle of the verb bilägga (“to append or attach”): bi- + lägga. The last part laga doesn't exist as a noun of its own, but is also found in inlaga (“petition”).

Noun

bilaga c

  1. an appendix (chapter at the end of a book)
  2. an attachment (enclosed matter in a letter or e-mail)
