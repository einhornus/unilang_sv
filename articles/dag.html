dag (154)


Etymology

From Old Swedish dagher, from Old Norse dagr, from Proto-Germanic *dagaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰegʷʰ- (“to burn, to be illuminated”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /dɑː(ɡ)/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

dag c

  1. a day
  2. a day, the period of time between sunrise and sunset, daytime
