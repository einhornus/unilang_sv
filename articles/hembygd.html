hembygd (73164)


Etymology

From hem (“home”) + bygd (“village, rural region”).

Noun

hembygd c

  1. village or broader rural area where one grew up
    Synonyms: födelseort, hemort, hemstad, hemtrakt
  2. something that relates to the local rural area
