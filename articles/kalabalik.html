kalabalik (58169)


Etymology

From Turkish kalabalık (“crowd, crowded”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kalabaˈliːk/
  • Rhymes: -iːk

Noun

kalabalik c

  1. a tumult, a riot, a disorder, chaos
    Kalabaliken i Bender
    the skirmish at Bender (a historic event on February 1, 1713)
