utfärda (22818)


Etymology

Related to the verb färdas (“to travel”) and the nouns färd (“journey”) and utfärd (“expedition”), all based on the verb fara (“to fare, to travel”).

Verb

utfärda (present utfärdar, preterite utfärdade, supine utfärdat, imperative utfärda)
