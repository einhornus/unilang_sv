sund (14649)

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Etymology 2

From Old Norse sund, from Proto-Germanic *sundą, from Proto-Indo-European *sm̥tóm.

Noun

sund n

  1. sound, strait (narrow water)
Usage notes
  • Used alone, sundet refers to Öresund between Denmark and Sweden.
