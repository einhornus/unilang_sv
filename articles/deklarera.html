deklarera (25798)


Etymology

From Latin dēclārāre,[1] present active infinitive of dēclārō, from dē- + clārō. Cognate with Norwegian deklarere, Norwegian Nynorsk deklarera, deklarera, Danish deklarere, German deklarieren, Dutch declareren, English declare, French déclarer, Italian dichiarare, Portuguese declarar, Romanian declara, Spanish declarar, Catalan declarar, Galician declarar and Occitan declarar.

Verb

deklarera (present deklarerar, preterite deklarerade, supine deklarerat, imperative deklarera)

  1. to declare
  2. to file for tax returns (see självdeklaration)
