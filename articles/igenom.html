igenom (489)


Etymology

From Old Norse í gegnum.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɪˈjeːnɔm/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

igenom

  1. through
    min ansökan gick igenom
    my application went through (the controls and was accepted)

Preposition

igenom

  1. through, all through, throughout; a synonym or alternative form of genom
