Verb

titta (present tittar, preterite tittade, supine tittat, imperative titta)

  1. (without any particle) to look; keep one's eyes open as to be able to see
    Titta inte! Blunda!
    Don't look! Close your eyes!
  2. (with an unstressed "på") to look (to try to see something)
    Att titta på tv.
    To watch TV.
  3. (with a stressed "på") to watch something happen; to be a spectator
    Jag fick inte hjälpa honom, utan bara titta på när han försökte.
    I wasn't allowed to help him, only watch him try.
