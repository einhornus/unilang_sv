handelshögskola (114869)


Etymology

From handel (“trade”) + -s- + högskola (“university college”).

Noun

handelshögskola c

  1. school of economics, school of business administration, school of economics and business administration; A university specialized in business administration and/or economics.
    Min flickvän läser på handelshögskola. - My girlfriend is studying at a school of economics and business administration.

Usage notes

  • Despite its name, a handelshögskola is a full-fledged university.
