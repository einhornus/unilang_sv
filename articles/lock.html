lock (14024)


Etymology

From Old Norse lok.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

lock c or n

  1. (chiefly in the plural) a lock of hair
  2. a cover, a lid
  3. popping (as when ears pop)[1]
    Få lock för örat.
    Be deafened.
  4. a (thin) board that covers the gap between panel boards
  5. call, lure (uninflected, from the verb locka)
    med lock och pock
