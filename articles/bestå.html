bestå (20123)


Etymology

be- + stå

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oː

Verb

bestå (present består, preterite bestod, supine bestått, imperative bestå)

  1. to consist (of parts), to be made of
    Livet består av 29 000 dagar
    Life consists of 29,000 days
  2. to (continue to) exist, to persist, to prevail, to stand (the test of time) (rhymes with its opposite: förgå)
    Åsikter förgår men värderingar består.
    Opinions vanish but values persist.
