sprit (2555)


Etymology

From French ésprit (compare English sprite), from Old French esprit, from Latin spiritus (“air, breath”).

Pronunciation

  • audio

Noun

sprit c (uncountable)

  1. spirits; liquor
  2. alcohol in general, chiefly as a solvent
