från (66)


Etymology

From Old Swedish frān, extended (with unexplained -n) from older Old Swedish frā, a proclitic form of Old Swedish fram, from Proto-Germanic *fram (“from”), from Proto-Germanic *promo- (“forth, forward”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /froːn/, /frɔn/
