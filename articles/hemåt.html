hemåt (11259)


Etymology

hem + åt, also written as separate words, attested as such (hem åt) in the 1541 Bible translation, Numeri 32:18

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb

hemåt

  1. home (as a direction), homebound
    ni kan gå hem när ni tröttnar, jag börjar gå hemåt nu
    you can go home when you get tired, I'm heading home now

Synonyms

  • hem
