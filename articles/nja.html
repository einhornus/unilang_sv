nja (4310)


Etymology

Blend of nej (“no”), and ja (“yes”). Compare German jein.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /njɑː/, /nja/

Interjection

nja

  1. Used to give a hesitant or uncertain answer or (dis)agreement; neither yes nor no; both yes and no; "well …", leaning slightly towards the negative.

Related terms

  • ja
  • nej
  • tja
