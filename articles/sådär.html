sådär (1609)


Etymology

så + där, in sense 1 more often written as separate words

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -æːr

Adverb

sådär

  1. thus, in that way, like that, to that degree
    så där ska man inte göra
    that's how you're not supposed to do it
  2. approximately, roughly, around
    bilen körde sådär 50 km/h
    the car went around 50 km/h
  3. so-so, neither well nor poorly
    Hur går det? − Det går sådär.
    How is it going? − It is going so-so.
