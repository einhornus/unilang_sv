vallmo (63826)


Etymology

Cognate with Danish valmue. From valen. The second part is cognate with German Mohn, Ancient Greek μήκων (mḗkōn) (Doric μάκων (mákōn)) and Church Slavonic макъ (makŭ) (Bulgarian, Russian, Serbo-Croatian мак).

Noun

vallmo c

  1. poppy
