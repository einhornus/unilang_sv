ohygglig (29890)


Etymology

o- + hygglig (“nice”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ʊˈhʏɡ.lɪ(ɡ)/
  • Unlike the vast majority of words prefixed with o-, the o is here a short vowel.

Adjective

ohygglig (comparative mer ohygglig, superlative mest ohygglig)

  1. terrible, atrocious, awful
