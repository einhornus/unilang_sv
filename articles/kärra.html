kärra (8091)


Etymology

Inherited from Old Swedish kärra, probably borrowed from Old Frisian [Term?], compare Middle Dutch kerre and Middle Low German karre, ultimately from Latin carrus. Doublet of russ.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -²æra

Noun

kärra c

  1. a cart, a carriage, a wagon
  2. (colloquial) car
