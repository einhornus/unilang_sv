hemort (51605)


Etymology

From hem (“home”) + ort (“inhabited place”).

Noun

hemort c

  1. place of residence
    Synonym: hemstad
  2. place of registered residence
  3. place of birth or where one grew up
    Synonyms: födelseort, födelsehemort, hemstad, hembygd
  4. (of boats) homeport
    See also: hemmahamn
