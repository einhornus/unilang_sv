hur (41)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hwaru, from neuter dative of Proto-Germanic *hwar

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /hʉːr/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ʉːr

Adverb

hur

  1. how
    Hur är det bra för oss?
    How is that good for us?

  • huruledes (archaic)
