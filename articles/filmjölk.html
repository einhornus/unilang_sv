filmjölk (116795)


Etymology

fil (“cream”) + mjölk (“milk”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈfiːlˌmjœlk/

Noun

filmjölk c

  1. a kind of fermented and soured milk

Usage notes

  • It is common to abbreviate the word to simply fil, but this can also denote a larger family of similar soured milk products.
