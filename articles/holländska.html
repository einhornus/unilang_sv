holländska (12925)


Adjective

holländska

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of holländsk.

Noun

holländska c (abbreviated holl.)

  1. (uncountable) Dutch language
  2. Dutchwoman

Usage notes

  • Holländska refers, strictly speaking, only to the people and the dialect from the Dutch province of Holland, but is nevertheless commonly used about anything Dutch.
