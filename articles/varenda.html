varenda (1029)


Etymology

From var + enda.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Determiner

varenda (neuter vartenda)

  1. every single
    Varenda dag, samma gamla historia!
    Every single day, same old story!
    Åt han upp varenda godisbit?
    Did he eat every single piece of candy?

Usage notes

Also colloquially used, inflected as an adjective, in the superlative as varendaste. Inflecting this determiner as an adjective is considered nonstandard. See also enda/endaste.
