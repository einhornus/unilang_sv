landkrabba (58330)


Etymology

land (“land”) + krabba (“crab”). Attested since 1788.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlandˌkrabːa/

Noun

landkrabba c

  1. (nautical, derogatory) A person unfamiliar with sea or seamanship; a landlubber.
    Vi har bara odugliga landkrabbor i den här besättningen!
    There are nothing but useless landlubbers in this crew!
