säkra (1251)


Adjective

säkra

  1. absolute singular definite and plural form of säker.

Verb

säkra (present säkrar, preterite säkrade, supine säkrat, imperative säkra)

  1. to secure, to guarantee
  2. to secure (a weapon, to lock it's trigger), to half-cock, to fasten
  3. to install an electrical fuse, to determine the dimension of electrical wiring
    byggnaden har säkrats för 25 A
    the building has a 25-amp fuse
