rapphöna (105242)


Etymology

From Old Swedish raphōns, from Middle Low German raphōn (“partridge”), possibly from Proto-Slavic *arębъ (“partridge”).

Noun

rapphöna c

  1. a partridge (any bird of the genera Perdix or Alectoris)
  2. a kind of horse carriage
