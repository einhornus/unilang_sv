speta (132130)


Etymology

Derivation of spett (“spit, skewer”), from Old Swedish spet, probably a borrowing from Middle Low German lance, spear.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²speːta/
  • Rhymes: -²eːta

Noun

speta c

  1. (colloquial) thin person
  2. (Southern) splinter (small piece of wood)
    Synonyms: sticka, spåga, spink
