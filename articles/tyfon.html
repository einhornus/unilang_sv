tyfon (44461)


Etymology

Cognate with Danish tyfon, German Typhon, French typhon, English typhoon, used in Swedish since 1771.

Noun

tyfon c

  1. a typhoon, a kind of strong whirlwind or storm
  2. a (trademarked brand of pressured air) siren
