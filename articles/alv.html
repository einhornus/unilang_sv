alv (23606)


Etymology

From Old Norse alfr, from Proto-Germanic *albiz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *albʰós.

Noun

alv c

  1. (mythology, fantasy) elf; a human-resembling, often magical, being
  2. subsoil
