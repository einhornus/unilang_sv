tappa (2213)


Etymology

From Old Norse tapa (“to lose”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

tappa (present tappar, preterite tappade, supine tappat, imperative tappa)

  1. to drop (something by mistake)
  2. to lose
  3. to tap; to draw off liquid from a vessel

Usage notes

  • (tap): more often as tappa upp.
