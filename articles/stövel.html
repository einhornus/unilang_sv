stövel (18236)


Etymology

From Old Swedish støvel, from Old Norse styfill, from Middle Low German stevele, stovele, from Italian stivale, from Medieval Latin aestivale.

Noun

stövel c

  1. a boot (shoe that covers part of the leg)
