väktare (6161)


Etymology

From Old Swedish vaktare, væktare, from Middle Low German wechter or German Wächter, equivalent to vakta + -are. Cognate with Norwegian Bokmål vekter and Norwegian Nynorsk vektar, see also Swedish vakt (“guard”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

väktare c

  1. member of security in pubs, malls etc.
  2. guardian, keeper
    De är stenens väktare.
    They are the guardians of the stone.
