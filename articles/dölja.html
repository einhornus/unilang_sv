dölja (2697)


Etymology

Compare Middle English diȝel, Icelandic dylja.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /dœl.ja/
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

dölja (present döljer, preterite dolde, supine dolt, imperative dölj)

  1. to conceal, to hide; to put out of sight or to keep secret

Usage notes

Gömma does in general denote that some extra effort went into the hiding, while dölja may mean that simply something obscures the view of the object.
