svälla (38161)


Etymology

From Old Swedish svælla, from Old Norse svella, from Proto-Germanic *swellaną. Compare Icelandic svella, English swell, Low German swellen, Dutch zwellen, German schwellen.

Verb

svälla (present sväller, preterite svällde, supine svällt, imperative sväll)

  1. to swell
