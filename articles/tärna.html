tärna (25968)


Etymology 1

From Old Norse þerna (“servant girl, maid”), from Old Saxon thiorna, from Proto-Germanic *þewernǭ.

Noun

tärna c

  1. a bridesmaid

Etymology 2

From Old Norse þerna (“tern”).

Noun

tärna c
