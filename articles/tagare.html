tagare (76274)


Etymology

taga + -are

Noun

tagare c

  1. a taker (someone who takes, instead of giving)
    Man måste inte vara antingen givare eller tagare.
    You need not be either a giver or a taker.

Usage notes

  • while uncommon on its own, this is a common ending in compounds
