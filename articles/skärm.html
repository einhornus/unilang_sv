skärm (14416)


Etymology

From Old Swedish skærmber, from Middle Low German scherm, from Proto-Germanic *skirmiz (“fur, pelt, covering, screen”).

Noun

skärm c

  1. screen; an informational viewing area
  2. (computer) monitor
  3. screen; a physical divider to block an area from view
  4. visor on a cap
