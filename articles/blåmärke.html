blåmärke (19881)


Etymology

blå + märke

Noun

blåmärke n

  1. bruise; a purplish mark on the skin due to leakage of blood from capillaries under the surface that have been damaged by a blow.
    Han har ett väldigt stort blåmärke
    He has a very big bruise
