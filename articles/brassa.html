brassa (42509)


Etymology

Related to brasa (“small fire”), comparing boastful behavior to the sound of roasting food. Compare the same development in German prassen (“to live extravagantly, boast”).[1]

Verb

brassa (present brassar, preterite brassade, supine brassat, imperative brassa)

  1. to boast
