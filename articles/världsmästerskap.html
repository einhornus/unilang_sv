världsmästerskap (69393)


Etymology

From värld + -s- + mästerskap.

Noun

världsmästerskap n

  1. a world championship (a title or a competition)

Usage notes

The word världsmästerskap is also used about what in English is called World cup in sports as football/soccer and ice hockey. World cup in sports such as skiing, which consists of a long series of point-giving races during the whole season, is called världscup in Swedish. These sports have world championships independent from their world cups.
