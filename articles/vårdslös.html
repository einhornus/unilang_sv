vårdslös (18337)


Etymology

From vård (“care”) + -s- + -lös.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²vɔrsˌløːs/, /²voːrdsˌløːs/

Adjective

vårdslös (comparative vårdslösare, superlative vårdslösast)

  1. careless (not giving sufficient attention or thought, especially concerning the avoidance of harm or mistakes)
