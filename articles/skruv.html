skruv (12922)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /skrʉːv/
  • Rhymes: -ʉːv

Noun

skruv c

  1. screw; a simple machine
  2. screw; (fastener)
  3. twist, turn; a rotation of the body while jumping (or diving)
  4. (ball sports) spin; a rotation of a ball while in air so that it does not follow a pure ballistic path
  5. (Finland) vint (card game)
