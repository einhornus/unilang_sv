fjärta (59914)


Etymology

From Old Norse frata, freta, from Proto-Germanic *fertaną, from Proto-Indo-European *perd-.

Verb

fjärta (present fjärtar, preterite fjärtade, supine fjärtat, imperative fjärta)

  1. to fart (to emit flatulent gases)
