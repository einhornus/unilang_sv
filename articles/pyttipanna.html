pyttipanna (69278)


Etymology

Possibly related to pytte- (“mini-”), i.e. something small put in a pan (”i panna”). Alternatively an imperative form of a dialectal word putta, pötta (“to put”), i.e. ”put things in a pan”.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pʏtɪ²pana/
  • Rhymes: -ana

Noun

pyttipanna c

  1. a dish consisting of fried diced meat and potatoes with onions
