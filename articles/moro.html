moro (51998)


Etymology

Likely from Swedish morgon, morron (compare Swedish god morgon).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmoro/, [ˈmo̞ro̞]
  • Rhymes: -oro
  • Syllabification: mo‧ro

Interjection

moro

  1. (colloquial) Hello, hi (used when meeting, sometimes when parting). Used especially in Häme (Tavastia) region.
