störa (2217)


Etymology

From German stören. From Old Norse staura.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /stœːra/
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

störa (present stör, preterite störde, supine stört, imperative stör)

  1. to disturb, to confuse or irritate, to interfere, to make noise
  2. to put down poles into the ground
