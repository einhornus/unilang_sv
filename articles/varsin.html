varsin (11330)


Etymology

var + sin, also written separately

Pronoun

varsin (n varsitt, p varsina)

  1. each (on their own)
    Barnen fick varsitt äpple.
    The children got an apple each.
    Efter skilsmässan sålde de huset och köpte varsin lägenhet.
    After the divorce, they sold the house and bought an apartment each.
    Han har fyra barnbarn. Hans båda söner har varsina två döttrar.
    He has four grandchildren. Both of his sons have two daughters each.
