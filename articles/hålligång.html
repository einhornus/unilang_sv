hålligång (58762)


Etymology

håll + igång (lit. hold (it) ongoing, approx. keep it up)

Noun

hålligång n (uncountable)

  1. (slang) An act or instance of partying or merrymaking, especially one of good quality or when compared to one of inferior quality.
    Festen började kanske trögt, men framåt midnatt så var det ett jävlans hålligång på dansgolvet!
    The party may have begun slowly, but at midnight there was some helluva great partying on the dance floor!
