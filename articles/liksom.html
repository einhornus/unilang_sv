liksom (1173)


Adverb

liksom

  1. almost
    • (Can we date this quote?) Lars Winnerbäck, Jag får liksom ingen ordning (single):
      Jag får liksom ingen ordning på mitt liv
      I can't seem to sort out my life

Conjunction

liksom

  1. as, as if, just like, as well as, in the same way as; also likasom
    Ty du skall bryta sönder deras bördors ok och deras skuldrors gissel och deras plågares stav, likasom i Midjans tid.
    For thou hast broken the yoke of his burden, and the staff of his shoulder, the rod of his oppressor, as in the day of Midian. (Isaiah 9:4)
    jag har sett filmsnuttar på Youtube, liksom bilder publicerade i turkiska tidningar
    I have seen film clips on Youtube, as well as pictures published in Turkish newspapers
