bus (7587)


Etymology

From the verb busa (“to do mischief”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [bʉ͍ːs]
  • Rhymes: -ʉːs

Noun

bus n (uncountable)

  1. very innocent mischief, prank
    Trick or Treat is often translated with Bus eller godis
  2. general noise or trouble made by gangs of youths
