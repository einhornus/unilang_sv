ryta (35220)


Etymology

From Old Swedish riūta, rȳta, from Old Norse hrjóta, from Proto-Germanic *hreutaną.

Verb

ryta (present ryter, preterite röt, supine rutit, imperative ryt)

  1. to bellow or roar
  2. to scream or shout angrily
