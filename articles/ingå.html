ingå (11017)


Etymology

in + gå

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oː

Verb

ingå (present ingår, preterite ingick, supine ingått, imperative ingå)

  1. to be included, to join, to belong, to follow without extra cost
    momsen ingår
    VAT is included
  2. to join, to be hired
    Han blef svensk undersåte och ingick vid Kungliga biblioteket i Stockholm
    He became a Swedish citizen and was hired at the Royal Library in Stockholm
  3. to enter (engagement, marriage or an agreement)
    Under året har ingåtts 77 äktenskap
    During the year 77 marriages have been entered
    För de stater, som ingått kapitulationer med Turkiet, ...
    For those states, which have entered agreement with Turkey, ...
  4. to address (a high authority), to submit a request
    Före riksdagen 1847–48 hade judarna ingått till kunglig majestät med begäran om...
    Before the parliament session of 1847–48, the Jews had addressed to His Royal Majesty with a request for...
