poäng (1546)


Etymology

From French point

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [poˈɛŋ], [po̯ɛŋ]
  • Rhymes: -ɛŋː

Noun

poäng c

  1. a point, a score
  2. a point, an argument (opinion which adds to the discussion)
  3. a point, a punch line (of a joke)
    Det stod en ko på ängen. Det var poängen.
    There stood a cow on the meadow. That's the point.
