släpa (6749)


Etymology

Borrowed from Middle Low German slēpen, from Old Saxon *slēpian, from Proto-West Germanic *slaipijan.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

släpa (present släpar, preterite släpade, supine släpat, imperative släpa)

  1. to drag (with difficulty) over a surface
  2. (reflexive) to drag oneself, move slowly
