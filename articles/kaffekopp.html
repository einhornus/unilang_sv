kaffekopp (45649)


Etymology

From kaffe (“coffee”) + kopp (“cup”).

Noun

kaffekopp c

  1. coffee cup; a cup for drinking coffee from
  2. coffee cup; a measure of about 1.5 dl. As a kitchen measure somewhat less than an English cup.
