bäva (57977)


Etymology

From Proto-Germanic *bibāną (“to quake, shiver”).

Verb

bäva (present bävar, preterite bävade, supine bävat, imperative bäva)

  1. to tremble, quiver, shudder
    Synonyms: darra, skälva, skaka, ruska
  2. to be uneasy or fearful
