kontrollera (1790)


Etymology

kontroll + -era

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

kontrollera (present kontrollerar, preterite kontrollerade, supine kontrollerat, imperative kontrollera)

  1. to check (to inspect, examine)
  2. to have the control over
