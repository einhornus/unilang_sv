sprida (4113)


Etymology

From Old Swedish spridha, probably the same word as spredha, from Middle Low German sprēden, spreiden, from Proto-Germanic *spraidijaną (“to spread”). Cognate with Swedish spread and Swedish spreiten. Due to the absence of this word in Old Icelandic, the Swedish word as well as Danish sprede, Norwegian Bokmål spre, and Norwegian Nynorsk spreie are probably borrowed from Low German.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈspriːˌda/

Verb

sprida (present sprider, preterite spred or spridde, supine spridit or spritt, imperative sprid)

  1. to distribute, to spread
