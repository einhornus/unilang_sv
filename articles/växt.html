växt (5026)


Etymology

From Old Norse vǫxtr, from Proto-Germanic *wahstuz.

Noun

växt c

  1. plant, vegetable
  2. growth, size, length (about human and animal bodies and sometimes plants)
    vara liten i växten
    be a short person (literally: be small in (the) size/growth)
    stanna i växten
    stop growing (literally: stop in the growth)
