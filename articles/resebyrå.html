resebyrå (27674)


Etymology

resa (“to travel”) + byrå (“bureau, office, agency”); used in Swedish of furniture since 1766, used of agencies since 1894

Noun

resebyrå c

  1. a travel agency; a company that arranges personal travel and accommodation for travellers.
  2. (obsolete, furniture) a small chest of drawers for use when travelling
