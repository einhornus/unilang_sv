vädur (39094)


Etymology

From Old Norse veðr, from Proto-Germanic *weþruz.

Noun

vädur c

  1. a ram, a buck; the male of sheep
  2. (astronomy) (in definite form) Aries (a constellation)
  3. (astrology) (in definite form) Aries (an astrological sign in the Zodiac)
  4. a hydraulic ram (a kind of water pump)
  5. several races of rabbit with hanging ears
