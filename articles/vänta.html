vänta (118)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɛnːta/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

vänta (present väntar, preterite väntade, supine väntat, imperative vänta)

  1. to wait
    jag väntar på min vän
    I'm waiting for my friend
  2. to wait for, to expect
    hon väntar barn i april
    she is expecting a child (i.e. pregnant), to be born in April
