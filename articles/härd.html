härd (34413)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hærþer, from Middle Low German herd,[1] from Old Saxon heorþ, from Proto-West Germanic *herþ.

Noun

härd c

  1. stove, heater; an enclosed space in which fuel (usually wood) is burned to provide heating, usually for cooking
