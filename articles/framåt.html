framåt (1112)


Etymology

From fram + åt, also written as separate words, attested as such (fram åt) in the 1541 Swedish Bible translation (Ezekiel 1:12)

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

framåt (not comparable)

  1. forward, progressive, full of initiative, entrepreneurial, active
    Hon är en framåt elev och vill verkligen studera.
    She is an active pupil and really likes to study.

Adverb

framåt

  1. forward, ahead (as a direction)
    Det går framåt för den lilla staden.
    Things are moving forward for the small town.

Related terms

  • framåtskridande
  • framåtriktad

Preposition

framåt

  1. along, towards
    följ mig framåt vägen
    follow me along the road

Synonyms

  • bortåt
