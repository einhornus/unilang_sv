gråben (77075)


Etymology

Compound of grå (“grey”) + ben (“leg”). A noa-name, a euphemistic replacement of the word ulv or varg (which is in itself originally a noa-word).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɡroːˌbeːn/

Noun

gråben c or n

  1. (dated, euphemistic) wolf

Synonyms

  • tasse
  • ulv
  • varg
