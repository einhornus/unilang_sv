ålder (1566)


Etymology

From Old Swedish alder, from Old Norse aldr, from Proto-Germanic *aldrą, from Proto-Indo-European *al-, *h₂el- (“to grow”).

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

ålder c

  1. age; how old something or someone is
  2. an age, an era; a particular time period
