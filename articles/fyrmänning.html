fyrmänning (141225)


Etymology

From Old Swedish fiurmänninger, from fyra (“four”) and man (“man”), compare tremänning.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²fyːrˌmɛnɪŋ/

Noun

fyrmänning c

  1. third cousin (great-grandchild of a great-grandparent’s sibling)
    Synonyms: brylling, B-kusin
