hävd (54950)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hæfþ, from Old Norse hefð.

Noun

hävd c

  1. maintenance, keeping things in a good state
  2. tradition (memories that are maintained)
    Vi har av hävd alltid hållit årsmötet på en lördag.
    We have traditionally always held the general assembly on a Saturday.
  3. history (mostly in plural definite: hävderna)
    Denna dag kommer att gå till hävderna.
    This day will go down in history.
