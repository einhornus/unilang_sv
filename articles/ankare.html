ankare (21937)


Etymology

From Old Norse akkeri. Cognate with Icelandic akkeri.

Noun

ankare n

  1. an anchor (tool to hook a vessel into sea bottom)
  2. (Internet) anchor; an HTML/XHTML mark-up tag to define a position in a file, or a link to a URL.
