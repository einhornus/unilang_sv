byrå (9293)


Etymology

Borrowed from French bureau (“office”), from bure (“woolen cloth”). Thus originally a type of coarse woolen cloth used to cover a writing desk.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbyːˌrɔ/, /ˈbyːrɔ/, /bʏˈroː/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oː

Noun

byrå c

  1. a bureau, a dresser, a chest of drawers
  2. a bureau, an office, an agency

Usage notes

  • In some job titles, the prefix byrå- is translated to senior, e.g. byråassistent
