vederbörande (29714)


Etymology

past participle of vederböra.

Adjective

vederbörande

  1. (the) concerned (person)
Synonyms
  • hen

Usage notes

  • The word is often used as an alternative to hon or han ("he, she") in subordinate clauses, particularly in written language and fills the function of a pronoun. A newly-invented alternative is the pronoun hen.

Verb

vederbörande

  1. present participle of vederböra
