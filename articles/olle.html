olle (15677)


Etymology

From Olle, a nickname for Olof or Olov. Often said to be named after the military captain D. F. G. A. Ohlson in Jönköping, who introduced such sweaters to the army.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ʊlɛ/
  • Rhymes: -²ʊlɛ

Noun

olle c

  1. a type of long-sleeved knitted sweater
