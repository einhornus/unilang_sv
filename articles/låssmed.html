låssmed (33737)


Etymology

lås + smed, from Old Swedish lasasmidher.

Noun

låssmed c

  1. a locksmith, a craftsman who manufactures and repairs locks and doorknobs
  2. (obsolete, military) a craftsman who manufactures and repairs triggers for handguns and rifles
