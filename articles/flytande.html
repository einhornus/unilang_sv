flytande (4710)


Etymology

Present participle of flyta ("flow" or "float")

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

flytande (comparative mer flytande, superlative mest flytande)

  1. fluent
    Han pratar flytande engelska.
    He speaks fluent English.
  2. flowing, tending to flow
  3. fluid
  4. liquid, existing in the physical state of a liquid
  5. buoyant, floating
  6. floating, present participle of flyta; to float

Adverb

flytande (comparative mer flytande, superlative mest flytande)

  1. fluently
    Han pratar engelska flytande.
    He speaks English fluently.
  2. buoyantly
  3. flowingly

Verb

flytande

  1. present participle of flyta
