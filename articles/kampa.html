kampa (77120)


Etymology

Borrowed from Old Swedish kamber (whence modern Swedish kam, dialectal kamba), from Proto-Germanic *kambaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵómbʰos. Doublet of hammas.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈkɑmpɑ/, [ˈkɑmpɑ]
  • Rhymes: -ɑmpɑ
  • Syllabification: kam‧pa

Noun

kampa

  1. comb
  2. (anatomy) pecten
