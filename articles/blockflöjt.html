blockflöjt (93642)


Etymology

Calque of German Blockflöte, from block + flöjt. Cognate with Danish blokfløjte (whence also Greenlandic blokfløjte), Norwegian Bokmål blokkfløyte, Norwegian Nynorsk blokkfløyte and Icelandic blokkflauta.

Noun

blockflöjt c

  1. (music) recorder (woodwind musical instrument)
