krossa (2373)


Etymology

From Old Swedish krossa, krosa, krusa, probably from Middle Low German krossen (“to break”).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

krossa (present krossar, preterite krossade, supine krossat, imperative krossa)

  1. to crush; to press or bruise between two hard bodies until the point of breaking
  2. to crush; to overwhelm (opposition, resistance)
  3. (colloquial) to crush; overcome completely
