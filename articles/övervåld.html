övervåld (35788)


Etymology

From Old Swedish ivirvald, from Old Norse yfirvald, equivalent to över (“over”) + våld (“violence”). Cognate with Norwegian Bokmål overvold, Danish overvold, Icelandic yfirvald (“authority”), German Obergewalt (“supremacy”).

Noun

övervåld n (uncountable)

  1. excessive violence, too much violence than what is necessary
    Polisen har anklagats för bruk av övervåld.
    The police has been accused of using excessive violence.
