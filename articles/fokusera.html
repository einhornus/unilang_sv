fokusera (3286)


Etymology

fokus + -era

Verb

fokusera (present fokuserar, preterite fokuserade, supine fokuserat, imperative fokusera)

  1. to focus (to adjust a lens, an optical instrument)
  2. to focus, to concentrate (attention on a subject, resources to a goal)
