aldrig (89)


Etymology

From Old Norse aldri, aldrigi. Cognate with Icelandic aldrei, Norwegian Nynorsk aldri and Danish aldrig.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaldri(ɡ)/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adverb

aldrig (not comparable)

  1. never
    Jag har aldrig sett det förr. ― I've never seen that before.
    Malin ljuger aldrig. ― Malin never lies.
    Antonym: alltid
