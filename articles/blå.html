blå (1288)


Etymology

From Old Norse blár (“blue”), from Proto-Germanic *blēwaz (“blue”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /bloː/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

blå (comparative blåare, superlative blåast)

  1. blue
  2. (politics) of or pertaining to conservatives or liberals in European politics
