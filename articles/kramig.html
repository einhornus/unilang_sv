kramig (123988)


Etymology

From kram or krama + -ig.

Adjective

kramig (comparative kramigare, superlative kramigast)

  1. (informal) huggy (fond of hugging, tending to hug)

Usage notes

  • The adjective kramig usually has neutral or positive overtones (unlike kramsjuk).

Synonyms

  • (huggy): kramsjuk, kelsjuk

Related terms

  • kram
  • krama
