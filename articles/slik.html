slik (291948)


Etymology

From Old Swedish slīker, from Old Norse slíkr, from Proto-Germanic *swalīkaz.

Adjective

slik (not comparable)

  1. (dated) similar, such
    slika krav borde gälla för alla
    such requirements should hold (apply, be valid) for everybody
