stava (8545)


Etymology

From Old Norse stafa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈstɑː.va/
  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

stava (present stavar, preterite stavade, supine stavat, imperative stava)

  1. to spell; to write the individual letters of a word in the order that matches the rules of how that particular word should be written; to give the correct spelling of a word
    Hur stavar du ditt förnamn?
    How do you spell your first name?
