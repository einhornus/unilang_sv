gök (34438)


Etymology

From Old Swedish gø̄ker, from Old Norse gaukr, from Proto-Germanic *gaukaz.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /jøːk/

Noun

gök c

  1. cuckoo; a bird
  2. (slang) penis
  3. (colloquial) liquor, of a mouthful size
    Synonyms: sup, snaps, nubbe, hutt
