lyra (13678)


Noun

lyra c

  1. a lyre (a stringed musical instrument)
  2. a European pollock (Pollachius pollachius)
    Synonyms: lyrtorsk, bleksej (regional), bleka
  3. (brännboll) a catch without the ball having touched the ground
