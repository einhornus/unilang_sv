trut (21273)


Etymology

From Old Swedish thrutin, from Old Norse þrútna. Cognate with English throat (from Old English þrote).

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ʉːt

Noun

trut c

  1. Any of several species of large gulls.
  2. (slang) mouth
