snygg (856)


Etymology

From Old Norse snøggr, from Proto-Norse [script needed] (*snaggwu-). Compare dialectal snögg and Icelandic snöggur.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

snygg (comparative snyggare, superlative snyggast)

  1. handsome, good-looking (of a person)
  2. clean
  3. (slang) cool
