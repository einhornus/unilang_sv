nämen (5998)


Etymology

Contraction of nä, pronunciational spelling of nej (“no”), and men (“but”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /nɛmɛn/
  • Not stressed, stress comes on a word later in the sentence. In the usage exampel the only word stressed is du.

Interjection

nämen

  1. Expresses strong surprise, often positive
    Nämen, är du här?
    Oh, you're here?
