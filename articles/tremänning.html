tremänning (95494)


Etymology

From Old Swedish þræmænninger, from þrir (“three”) (Swedish tre) + maþer (“man”) (Swedish man) + -inger (Swedish -ing).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²treːˌmɛnɪŋ/

Noun

tremänning c

  1. (dialectal) second cousin (grandchild of a grandparent’s sibling)
