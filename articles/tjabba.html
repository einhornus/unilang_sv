tjabba (120208)


Etymology

From tja + ba, a colloquial pronunciation of bara. See also tjänare.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɕaba/

Interjection

tjabba

  1. (colloquial) An informal greeting.
    • 2016, Viveca Lärn, Eddie och Johanna:
      ”Tjabba Eddie!” sa han. Eddie blinkade och svalde. ”Tjabba Anders!” viskade han.
      ”Hi there Eddie!”, he said. Eddie blinked and swallowed. ”Hi there Anders!”, he whispered.
