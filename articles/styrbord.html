styrbord (7580)


Etymology

From styra (“steer”) + bord (“bord, side of ship”), the side which the steering oar was located, i.e. right.

Adverb

  1. in starboard (right) direction of a vessel
    Antonym: babord

Noun

styrbord n

  1. starboard (right) side of a vessel
    Antonym: babord
