hygglig (8030)


Etymology

From hygga + -lig. Cognate with Danish hyggelig, Norwegian Bokmål hyggelig, Norwegian Nynorsk hyggeleg, hyggjeleg and Dutch heuglijk.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈhʏɡˌlɪ(ɡ)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

hygglig (comparative hyggligare, superlative hyggligast)

  1. quite good
  2. (about a person) nice, kind
