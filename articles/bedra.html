bedra (8398)


Etymology

From earlier bedraga, from Old Swedish bedragha, from Middle Low German bedregen, from Old Saxon bidriogan, from Proto-West Germanic *bidreugan. Equivalent to be- + dra

Pronunciation

  • Rhymes: -ɑː

Verb

bedra (present bedrar, preterite bedrog, supine bedragit, imperative bedra)

  1. to deceive
