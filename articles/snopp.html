snopp (8700)


Etymology

From the verb snoppa (to remove small petrution)

Noun

snopp c

  1. (colloquial, neutral) penis, willy
    Synonyms: penis, pille, pillesnopp, Petter-Nicklas
    Antonym: snippa
  2. (horse marking) snip
  3. (colloquial) small cut off part, stem
    Synonyms: stump, blast, stjälk
