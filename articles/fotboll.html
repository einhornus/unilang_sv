fotboll (2533)


Etymology

From fot (“foot”) + boll (“ball”), a calque of English football.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²fuː(t)bɔl/

Noun

fotboll c

  1. soccer (US), football (UK); a team sport
  2. football; the ball used in any game called football/soccer.
