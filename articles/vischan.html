vischan (21310)


Etymology

From Tavringer Romani vissj, vässj (“forest”), from Romani weš, vesh (“forest”), a borrowing from Mazanderani ویشه (višé, “forest”), compare Persian بیشه (biše, “forest”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²vɪɧan/

Noun

vischan c (definite)

  1. (colloquial, somewhat derogatory) the boondocks, the backwaters

Usage notes

  • Only used in the definite form, usually in the construction på vischan.
