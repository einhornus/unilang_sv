brevduva (60251)


Etymology

From brev (“letter (written communication)”) + duva (“dove, pigeon”) (cognate with Danish brevdue, Icelandic bréfdúfa, Norwegian Bokmål brevdue, Norwegian Nynorsk brevdue, German Brieftaube, German Low German Breefduuv).

Noun

brevduva c

  1. homing pigeon (short form: homer), racing pigeon (a variety of domesticated rock dove (w:rock dove; English Name Updates: [[1]]))
