bestånd (72702)


Etymology

From the verb bestå

Noun

bestånd n

  1. a collection (of books in a library), a group (of plants in a garden), a set (of objects)
  2. (uncountable) continued existence
    Fonden avser att garantera klubbens fortsatta bestånd.
    The fund is intended to guarantee the club's continued existence.
