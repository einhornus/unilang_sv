emellertid (16973)


Etymology

i + medel + tid, at least since 1530: i midhell tidh, same as Danish imidlertid, Middle Low German middeler tit, similar to English meanwhile. Already in the 1580s, the use started to shift from time (= meanwhile) to cause (= however).

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -iÀêd

Adverb

emellertid

  1. (archaic) meanwhile, during the same time
  2. however

Conjunction

emellertid
