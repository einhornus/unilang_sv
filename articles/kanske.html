kanske (90)


Etymology

From kan (“can, may”) + ske (“happen, occur”), less commonly used similar constructions include kanhända, måhända and archaic måske. Compare Elfdalian kanstji, Norwegian kanskje, Icelandic kannski and Faroese kanska.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²kanɧɛ/, (Central Swedish) [²kanʂɛ], [²kanɧɛ]

Adverb

kanske (not comparable)

  1. maybe, possibly, perhaps
    Synonyms: måhända, kanhända, måske (archaic)
