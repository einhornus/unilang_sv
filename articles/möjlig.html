möjlig (6374)


Etymology

From Old Swedish møgheliker, from Old Norse mǫguligr, from Middle Low German mȫgelîk.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈmøjli(ɡ)/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

möjlig (comparative möjligare, superlative möjligast)

  1. possible
    så snart som möjligt
    as soon as possible
    i möjligaste mån
    as much as possible
