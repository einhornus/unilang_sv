räka (22347)


Etymology

Borrowing from a West Slavic language, ultimately from Proto-Slavic *rakъ (“crayfish”).

Noun

räka c

  1. a shrimp, a prawn
    På sommaren äter vi gärna räkor.
    We like to eat shrimp during the summer.
    Jag såg en räka på stranden.
    I saw a shrimp on the beach.
