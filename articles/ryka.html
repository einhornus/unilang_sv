ryka (37757)


Etymology

From Old Swedish riūka, from Old Norse rjúka, from Proto-Germanic *reukaną. See also rök.

Verb

ryka (present ryker, preterite rök or rykte, supine rykit or rykt, imperative ryk)

  1. (intransitive) to emit smoke
  2. disappear, vanish
