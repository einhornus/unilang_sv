inställning (6024)


Etymology

Calque of German Einstellung[1] or inställa + -ing[2]. Compare Danish indstilling, Norwegian Bokmål innstilling, German Einstellung, Dutch instelling.

Pronunciation

Noun

inställning c

  1. attitude, position (general approach to or opinion about a particular matter)
  2. (technology) setting (level or placement that a mechanical or electronic control is adjusted to)
