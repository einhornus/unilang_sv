vittu (61327)


Etymology

From Proto-Norse. Compare Swedish fitta and Old Norse fytta. Cognate to Estonian vitt, Ingrian vittu, and Veps vitõińe.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈʋitːu/, [ˈʋit̪ːu]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -itːu
  • Syllabification: vit‧tu

Noun

vittu

  1. (vulgar, slang) cunt, pussy.
  2. (vulgar) An intensifier, roughly equivalent to "fucking" or "fuck".
    Vitun idiootti!
    Fucking idiot!
    Mitä vittua?
    What the fuck?
  3. (vulgar) A place that is not here, used in certain expressions of place; fuck, hell.
    Missä vitussa sinä olet?
    Where the hell are you?
    painua / mennä / suksia vittuun ― to fuck off
    Painu vittuun!
    Fuck off!

Usage notes

  • Considered as, if not more, vulgar than fuck in English.
  • As the words kettu (“fox”), pottu (“potato”) and hattu (“hat”) rhyme with vittu, they may be used as a replacement for vittu and its derivatives to make the language more decent:
    vituttaa → hatuttaa / ketuttaa / potuttaa
    vittuilla → kettuilla / hattuilla / pottuilla
    vitutus → hatutus / ketutus / potutus.
