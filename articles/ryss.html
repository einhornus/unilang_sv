ryss (10821)


Etymology 1

From Old Swedish ryz, ryze. [1]

Noun

ryss c

  1. a Russian, male person from Russia
  2. (derogatory, archaic) a rude, violent, fierce or warlike person (traits that have been considered typical for Russians).[1]
