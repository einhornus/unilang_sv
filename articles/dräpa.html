dräpa (15594)


Etymology

From Old Swedish dræpa, Old Norse drepa (“to hit; to kill”), from Proto-Germanic *drepaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dhrebh-.

Verb

dräpa (present dräper, preterite dräpte, supine dräpt, imperative dräp)

  1. (poetic) to kill, to commit manslaughter
