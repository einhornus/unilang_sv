upphovsrätt (64815)


Etymology

upphov + rätt, like German Urheberrecht

Noun

upphovsrätt c

  1. copyright; an author's right; the right to a work of authorship
    Michael Jackson ägde patent, upphovsrätter och varumärken i en omfattning som är unik för en enskild artist.
    Michael Jackson owned patents, copyrights and trademarks to an extent that is unparalleled for an individual artist.
  2. copyright law; the field of law studies pertaining to copyright

Usage notes

  • Built in is the ambiguity of the word rätt (“right”), which can mean both an individual right (rättighet) and law studies (juridik). This makes "upphovsrättsjuridik" a tautology.
