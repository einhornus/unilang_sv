punkt (2485)


Etymology

From Latin punctum through Old Norse punktr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /pɵŋ(k)t/

Noun

punkt c

  1. a period, a full stop
  2. an item (on a list or an agenda)
  3. (typography) a point; size of a font
  4. (geometry) a point
  5. a dot; one of the two symbols used in Morse code
