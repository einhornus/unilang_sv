taskspelare (131264)


Etymology

Used in Swedish since 1732. From German Taschenspieler, also found in Danish taskenspiller, a performer who pulls things from or puts them into a Tasche (“bag, pocket”), aus der Tasche spielen.

Noun

taskspelare c

  1. a jester, a joker, a (less serious) actor, an artist, a performer
