röka (1751)


Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

röka (present röker, preterite rökte, supine rökt, imperative rök)

  1. to smoke; to inhale the smoke of burning tobacco
  2. to treat foods with smoke to reduce the risk of it getting spoiled
  3. to smoke (e.g. an animal out of its hiding)
