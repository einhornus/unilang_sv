bekämpa (5091)


Etymology

From German bekämpfen, equivalent to be- + kämpa. Cognate with Danish bekæmpe, Norwegian Bokmål bekjempe and Dutch bekampen.

Pronunciation

Verb

bekämpa (present bekämpar, preterite bekämpade, supine bekämpat, imperative bekämpa)
