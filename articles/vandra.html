vandra (5767)


Etymology

From Old Swedish vandra, from Middle Low German wanderen, from Old Saxon *wandrōn, from Proto-Germanic *wandrōną.

Pronunciation

Verb

vandra (present vandrar, preterite vandrade, supine vandrat, imperative vandra)
