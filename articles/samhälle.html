samhälle (4306)


Etymology

From Old Norse samheldi, equivalent to sam- + hålla.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [samˈhɛlːˌɛ]
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

samhälle n

  1. a society; a long-standing group of people sharing cultural aspects
  2. a society, the sum total of all voluntary interrelations between individuals.
  3. a society; the people of one’s country or community
  4. a community, a place
  5. a large village, a small town (especially if relying heavily on a neighboring larger town for places to work etc.)
  6. a colony of animals (bees, ants)
