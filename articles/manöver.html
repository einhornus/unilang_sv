manöver (8118)


Etymology

Borrowed from French manœuvre, from Old French maneuvre, from Latin manuopera, derived from manu (ablative of manus (“hand”) and opera (plural of opus (“work”)). Compare English manoeuvre, manual, operation and operate in English.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /manˈøːvɛr/

Noun

manöver c

  1. A manoeuvre
  2. A move, manoeuvre
