gaffla (73997)


Etymology

gaffel + -a

Verb

gaffla (present gafflar, preterite gafflade, supine gafflat, imperative gaffla)

  1. (colloquial, intransitive) to chat, to quibble, to argue
    vad sitter ni och gafflar om?
    what are you arguing about?
  2. (transitive, computing, slang) to fork (a process or a project into several instances)
