inhemsk (28332)


Etymology

From in- + hem + -sk.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

inhemsk (not comparable)

  1. domestic (from the own country)
    Undvik främmande ord när det finns en adekvat inhemsk vokabulär.
    Avoid foreign words in the presence of an adequate domestic vocabulary. (falsely attributed to Gunnar Sträng, who actually said: adekvat svensk vokabulär)
