sosse (128231)


Etymology

Pronunciation spelling of a clipping of Socialdemokraterna, socialdemokrat or socialist + -e.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsʊˌsɛ/, /ˈsɔˌsɛ/

Noun

sosse c

  1. (colloquial, politics, often derogatory) A social democrat.

Usage notes

Often used as a prefix in compounds forming words such as: sossegöra (“things only social democrats do”), sosseyrke (“social democrat job”) and sosselivsstil (“social democrat livestyle”).
