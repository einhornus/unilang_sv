ria (40315)


Etymology

Probably from Finnish riihi (“kiln, grain drying cabin”).

Noun

ria c

  1. a dryer, a kiln to dry corn; a traditional Finnish/Scandinavian wooden building used for indoor drying of corn

  1. to dry corn in a kiln
