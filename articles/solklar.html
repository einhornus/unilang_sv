solklar (67332)


Etymology

From sol + klar. Compare similar constructions with Danish solklar (“lit up and clear by the sun”), Norwegian Bokmål solklar (“lit up and clear by the sun”), Norwegian Nynorsk solklar, solklår, German sonnenklar and Dutch zonneklaar.

Adjective

solklar (comparative mer solklar, superlative mest solklar)

  1. super obvious
    Domaren missade en solklar straff som tyvärr avgjorde matchen.
    The referee missed to see a super obvious penalty that unfortunately decided the game.
