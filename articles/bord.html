bord (1699)


Etymology

From Old Norse borð, from Proto-Germanic *burdą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /buːɖ/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

bord n

  1. a table (a piece of furniture)
  2. (nautical) a plank used in the side of a hull
