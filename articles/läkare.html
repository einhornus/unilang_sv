läkare (924)


Etymology

From late Old Swedish lǣkiare, from earlier Old Swedish lǣkir, from (east) Old Norse *lækir (cf. Old West Norse læknir), from Proto-Germanic *lēkijaz. Equivalent to läka + -are.
