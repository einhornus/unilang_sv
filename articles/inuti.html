inuti (2254)


Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Preposition

inuti

  1. in, within, inside
    Jag lade frimärkena inuti kuvertet så att du inte tappar bort dem = I put the stamps inside the envelope so that you won't lose them
  2. on the inside
    Undrar hur en tennisboll ser ut inuti? = Wonder what a tennis ball looks like on the inside?

Usage notes

Implies more stress on the location as within/on the inside of something—usually something small and/or inaccessible—than does i, which also can have meanings closer to "at" (i skolan = at school), or inne i which denotes a position within, but still with plenty of space and/or easy access (such as buildings).

Synonyms

  • i
  • inne i
