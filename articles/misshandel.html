misshandel (7623)


Etymology

Noun form of misshandla, cognate with Middle High German Missehandel, and German Misshandel (wrongdoing, misdeed), originally in the now obsolete sense of wrongdoing, misdeed, but since 1840 in the sense of physical abuse of people.

Noun

  1. abuse (of another person; physically, mentally, or sexually; but not drug abuse)
