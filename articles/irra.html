irra (40709)


Etymology

From German irren (“wander, stray, err, mistake”, verb), from Proto-Germanic *irzijaną (“to delude, mislead”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɪrˌa/

Verb

irra (present irrar, preterite irrade, supine irrat, imperative irra)

  1. to wander, stray (usually with omkring)
