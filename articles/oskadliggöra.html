oskadliggöra (20809)


Etymology

oskadlig + göra, used since 1820

Verb

oskadliggöra (present oskadliggör, preterite oskadliggjorde, supine oskadliggjort, imperative oskadliggör)

  1. to render harmless or innocuous; to neutralize (a poison), to disarm (an explosive mine), to put (a weapon) out of action, to put (a criminal) out of the way of doing harm
