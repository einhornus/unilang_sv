synas (7542)


Etymology

From Old Norse sýnask (“to appear”), from sýna.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

synas (present syns, preterite syntes, supine synts, imperative syns)

  1. have as appearance; to appear, to seem
  2. to be visible
  3. to meet each other (by happenstance)
  4. infinitive passive of syna.
  5. present tense passive of syna.
