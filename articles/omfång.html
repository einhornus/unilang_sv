omfång (55051)


Etymology

From German Umfang, used since 1639.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɔmˌfɔŋ/

Noun

omfång n

  1. extent, register, reach, size, width (originally: circumference)
    i det omfång som...
    to that extent which...
    hennes röst har ett stort omfång
    her voice has a large register
