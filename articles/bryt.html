bryt (2608)


Noun

bryt n

  1. (slang) slang translation of the English noun break, based on the verb bryta, corresponding to various Swedish nouns ending in -brott.
    1. sammanbrott, a mental breakdown
    2. utbrott, an outburst of anger, a fit
      min man som får stå ut med mina bryt
      my husband, who has to put up with my fits
    3. avbrott, an interruption, intermission, or ending
      Man behöver ett bryt från vardagen
      You need a break from the daily routine
    4. place where waves break, breaking waves, suitable for surfing
      brädan tar sig fram mellan bryten
      the board moves between the breaks
  2. definite singular of bry

Usage notes

  • Bryten/brytet is also a common misspelling for bruten, brutet, brutit.
