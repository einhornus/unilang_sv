sköldpadda (11559)


Etymology

sköld (“shield”) + padda (“toad”), from Old Swedish skioldpadda, a calque of Low German schildepadde, schiltpadde; compare with Norwegian Nynorsk skjelpadde, Danish skildpadde, Norwegian Bokmål skilpadde and Dutch schildpad.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɧœldˌpada/
  • Hyphenation: sköld‧pad‧da

Noun

sköldpadda c

  1. turtle
  2. tortoise
