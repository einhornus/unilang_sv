sött (2600)


Etymology

From Old Norse sœtr, from Proto-Germanic *swōtuz, from Proto-Indo-European *sweh₂dus. Compare Icelandic sætur (“sweet”) and its neuter form sætt.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sœt/

Adjective

sött

  1. absolute indefinite neuter form of söt.

Adverb

sött (not comparable)

  1. sweet, sweetly
  2. sweet; as of sugar
