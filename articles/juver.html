juver (59195)


Etymology

From Old Swedish iūver, iūgher, from Old Norse júr, júgr, from Proto-Germanic *euduraz, from Proto-Indo-European *H₁ewHdʰ-r̥- (“udder”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈjʉvɛr/

Noun

juver n

  1. an udder (part of domestic milk-giving animal that expresses milk)
