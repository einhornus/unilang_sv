fabrikat (70467)


Etymology

fabrik + -at, cognate with German Fabrikat, used in Swedish since 1811.

Noun

fabrikat n

  1. a product; the result of manufacture, the output of factories
    företaget tillverkar husgeråd, verktyg och diverse fabrikat av trä
    the company manufactures kitchen utensils, tools, and various wooden products
  2. manufacture, make, fabric, a brand
    av svenskt fabrikat
    of Swedish making; made in Sweden
    Vår fabrik levererar dessutom stångbrotchar med vetenskaplig noggrannhet till alla slag av brotchmaskiner, oavsett fabrikat.
    Our factory also supplies linear broaches with scientific accuracy to all kinds of broaching machines, of any brand.
