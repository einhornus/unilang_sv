landsväg (33746)


Etymology

From land + -s- + väg.

Noun

landsväg c

  1. a highway, a main public road (most often used of older rural roads, not of motorways)

Usage notes

  • Not to be confused with landvägen (“the overland route”), as opposed to sjövägen (“by sea”).
