abbot (27041)


Etymology

From Old Swedish abbot, from Latin abbās, from Ancient Greek ἀββᾶς (abbâs), from Aramaic אבא (’abbā, “father”). Doublet of abbé.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈabːɔt/

Noun
