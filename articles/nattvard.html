nattvard (35118)


Etymology

From Old Swedish natvarþer, Old Norse náttverðr, from nátt (“night”) + verðr (“meal”). Cognate of Danish nadver.

Noun

nattvard c

  1. (Christianity) Holy Communion
  2. (historical) supper
