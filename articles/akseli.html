akseli (48131)


Etymology

Borrowed from Swedish axel (“axle, axis”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɑkseli/, [ˈɑks̠e̞li]
  • Rhymes: -ɑkseli
  • Syllabification: ak‧se‧li

Noun

akseli

  1. axle, shaft
  2. (geometry) axis (imaginary line around which an object spins or is symmetrically arranged)
    Maa pyörähtää kerran päivässä akselinsa ympäri.
    The Earth rotates once a day on its axis.
  3. (mathematics) axis (fixed one-dimensional figure forming part of the basis of a space)
  4. (psychiatry) axis (form of classification and description of mental disorders or disabilities)
  5. (botany) axis (main stem or central part about which organs or plant parts such as branches are arranged)
