trivsel (78460)


Etymology

Attested from 1891 according to Svenska Akademiens ordbok. From trivas (“to feel comfortable”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈtrɪfselː/ or /ˈtrɪvselː/

Noun

trivsel c

  1. A sense or state of comfort and well-being.
    Det uppstod en påtaglig trivsel efter att Filip bjudit alla på tårta.
    "There arose a considerable sense of well-being after Filip treated everyone to cake."
