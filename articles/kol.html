kol (6641)


Etymology

From Old Norse kol, from Proto-Germanic *kulą, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷol-, from *ǴwelH- (“to burn, shine”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /koːl/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Homophone: kål

Noun

kol n

  1. (uncountable) carbon
  2. (uncountable) coal, either rock or charcoal
  3. (countable) a piece of coal
