paff (35400)


Etymology

From German paff, baff (“bang”), from the stunned reaction one gets when firing a weapon.

Adjective

paff (comparative paffare, superlative paffast)

  1. (colloquial) stunned, speechless
    Synonyms: ställd, förbluffad
