börs (19946)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

börs c

  1. stock market, stock exchange, bourse
  2. purse, a bag for carrying money, (more generally) a hypothetical purse which contains someone's complete wealth
    Hans börs är välfylld.
    He is wealthy. (His purse is well filled.)
