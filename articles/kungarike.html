kungarike (8450)


Etymology

From Old Norse konungríki, from Proto-Germanic *kuningarīkiją. Equivalent to kung + -a- + rike. Compare Danish kongerige, Norwegian kongerike, Icelandic konungsríki, Dutch koninkrijk, German Königreich.

Noun

kungarike n

  1. a kingdom
