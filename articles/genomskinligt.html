genomskinligt (42920)


Adjective

genomskinligt

  1. absolute indefinite neuter form of genomskinlig.

Adverb

genomskinligt (comparative genomskinligare, superlative genomskinligast)

  1. translucently
  2. (colloquial) transparently
  3. in a clear manner, in a manner that makes it possible for others to understand what is happening
