daggmask (64628)


Etymology

dagg (“dew”) + mask (“worm”), used since 1753 in the current sense (Lumbricus terrestris), but earlier of other worms and snails (without a shell), compare English dewsnail.

Noun

daggmask c

  1. an earthworm (Lumbricus terrestris)
