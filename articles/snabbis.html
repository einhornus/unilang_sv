snabbis (17139)


Etymology

snabb + -is

Noun

snabbis c

  1. (colloquial) A quickie, something of short duration
    Kan jag få spela lite? ― Can I play a little?
    Okej, men bara en snabbis. ― Okay, but only a quickie.
  2. (colloquial, by extension) A quickie, a brief sexual encounter
