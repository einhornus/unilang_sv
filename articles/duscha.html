duscha (5342)


Etymology

dusch + -a

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²dɵɕa/, (south Sweden) /²dɵɧa/

Verb

duscha (present duschar, preterite duschade, supine duschat, imperative duscha)

  1. to shower (spray with)
  2. to shower; to clean oneself in a shower; take a shower / have a shower
