läsning (11601)


Etymology

läsa + -ning

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

läsning c

  1. reading (the activity of reading from text, whether silent or loud)
  2. recital (the activity of reading out loud, whether from text or memory)
  3. literature, something to read
    har du någon läsning med dig på kryssningen?
    did you bring anything to read on the cruise?
  4. an interpretation, a (different) understanding (of a text)
  5. a read, a fetch (from computer memory or data storage)
