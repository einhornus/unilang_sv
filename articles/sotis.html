sotis (38138)


Etymology

sot (“soot”) + -is, because soot is black and svartsjuk, which is the most common term, literally means black sick.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /¹suːtɪs/

Adjective

sotis (comparative mer sotis, superlative mest sotis)

  1. (colloquial) jealous
    Synonyms: svartsjuk, svartis (somewhat old-fashioned)

Declension

Invariable, only used predicatively.
