grina (16674)


Etymology

From Old Norse grína, from Proto-Germanic *grīnaną.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɡriːˌna/

Verb

grina (present grinar, preterite grinade, supine grinat, imperative grina)

  1. (colloquial, derogatory) to weep, to cry, to cry too much or for the wrong reason
  2. (dialectal, chiefly Scania and Finland) to laugh
