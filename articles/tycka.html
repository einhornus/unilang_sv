tycka (1684)


Etymology

From Old Swedish þykkia, þykia, from Old Norse þykkja, from Proto-Germanic *þunkijaną.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Verb

tycka (present tycker, preterite tyckte, supine tyckt, imperative tyck)

  1. to think of, to have an opinion about
  2. to opine, to publicly express an opinion (mostly without knowledge)
