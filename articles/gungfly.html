gungfly (117503)


Etymology

Compound of gunga (“to swing”) + fly (“quagmire”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²ɡɵŋˌflyː/

Noun

gungfly n

  1. swampy of vegetation; a quagmire
    Synonym: fly
  2. (figurative) a difficult situation; a predicament; a quagmire
