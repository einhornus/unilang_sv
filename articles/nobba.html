nobba (41328)


Etymology

From Tavringer Romani noba, nåbba, from German Rotwelsch nobis (“nothing”), of uncertain origin.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²nɔba/
  • Rhymes: -²ɔba

Verb

nobba (present nobbar, preterite nobbade, supine nobbat, imperative nobba)

  1. (colloquial) to reject, to say no
