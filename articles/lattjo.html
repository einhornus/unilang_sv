lattjo (69325)


Etymology

From Tavringer Romani lattjo (“good, fine”), Romani laćho (“good”), same root as in Sanskrit लक्ष्मी (lakṣmī, “good fortune, wealth”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlatɕʊ/

Adjective

lattjo (not comparable)

  1. (colloquial) fun

Synonyms

  • kul
  • skoj
  • rolig

Derived terms

  • lattja
