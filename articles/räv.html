räv (8437)


Etymology

From Old Norse refr, from Proto-Norse ᚱᛖᚹᚨᛉ (rewaz, “brown”).[1] (but compare Persian روباه (rubâh, “fox”), apparently from a separate root).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /rɛːv/

Noun

räv (c)

  1. a fox (a mammal)
  2. (dialectial) an old and experienced person
