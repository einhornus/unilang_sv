silver (3229)


Etymology

From Old Swedish silver, from Old Norse silfr, from Proto-Germanic *silubrą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈsɪlvɛr/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

silver n (uncountable)

  1. silver
  2. silver, coins of silver
  3. silver, cutlery of silver
  4. a silver medal, for 2nd place in a competition
