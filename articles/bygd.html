bygd (93785)


Etymology

From Old Swedish byghþ, from Old Norse byggð, from Proto-Germanic *biwwiþō.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈbʏɡːd/
  • Homophone: byggd

Noun

bygd c

  1. a village
  2. a region, a country district (tied together by common traditions)
