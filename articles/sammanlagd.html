sammanlagd (66290)


Etymology

From the past participle of the verb lägga samman

Adjective

sammanlagd (not comparable)

  1. added together, taken together; (counted as if) put in one joint pile
    De har en sammanlagd skuld om 10 000 kr.
    They have, taken together, a debt of 10 000 SEK.
