vackert (903)


Adjective

vackert

  1. absolute indefinite neuter form of vacker.

Adverb

vackert (comparative vackrare, superlative vackrast)

  1. beautifully
  2. (somewhat dated, not comparable) carefully; with great caution and care
  3. (dated, not comparable) sweet (chiefly as a response to positive news)
