bondfångare (53179)


Etymology

From bonde (“farmer”) + fångare (“catcher”)

Noun

bondfångare n

  1. confidence trickster, someone who exploits someones fatuity or credulity
  2. a type of Chinese finger trap made of woven birchbark
    Synonym: fingerfälla (finger trap)
