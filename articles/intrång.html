intrång (6531)


Etymology

From the verb intränga

Noun

intrång n

  1. an intrusion, a trespassing, an infringement

Usage notes

  • Swedish criminal law (Brottsbalken, chapter 4) makes a distinction between inbrott (“burglary, intrusion in a home”) and intrång (“intrusion in other buildings”)
