beslagen (142931)


Etymology

past participle of beslå.

Adjective

beslagen

  1. decorated, covered, dressed, having applications (of metal on wood, leather or textile)
    Portarna är beslagna med kopparplåt.
    The doors are covered with copper sheets.
  2. accused
    han var beslagen med lögn
    he was accused of a lie
