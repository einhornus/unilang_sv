nedre (9506)


Etymology

From Old Norse niðri.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

nedre

  1. lower
    nedre våningen
    the lower floor
  2. southern
    nedre Norrland
    southern Norrland

Usage notes

  • The word looks like the comparative of an adjective, but ner, ned, neder (“down”) are adverbs, not adjectives.

Synonyms

  • undre
