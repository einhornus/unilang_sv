trist (3501)


Etymology

Borrowed from French triste, from Latin tristis.

Pronunciation

  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

trist (comparative tristare, superlative tristast)

  1. boring
  2. not funny, bad, a pity,...
    Det var trist att höra att din kanin dött
    I’m sorry to hear that your bunny died
