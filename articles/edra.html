edra (34544)


Pronoun

edra

  1. (archaic) your; plural of eder; standard Swedish era is a contracted form of this
    • Isaiah 55:3
      Böjen edra öron hit och kommen till mig; hören, så får eder själ leva.
      Incline your ear, and come unto me: hear, and your soul shall live.

Usage notes

Still used frequently in the phrase på edra platser, färdiga, gå (“on your marks, get set, go”).
