flummig (47709)


Etymology

Of unknown origin, possibly from English flummery (“empty or meaningless talk”), borrowed from Welsh llymru (“a jelly derived from oatmeal”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /flɵ.mɪɡ/

Adjective

flummig

  1. dopey, hazy
  2. frivolous, nonsensical, silly
  3. intoxicated, high
