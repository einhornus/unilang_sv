bildning (29232)


Etymology

bilda + -ning, related to German Bildung

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

bildning c

  1. a formation, a buildup (geologic, biologic, organizational)
  2. (uncountable) (the level of) education
    han har varken bildning eller uppförande
    he has neither education nor manners
