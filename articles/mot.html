mot (135)


Etymology

From Old Norse mót, from Proto-Germanic *mōtą, *gamōtą.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /muːt/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

mot n

  1. (chiefly west Sweden) interchange; a large junction where two or more roads meet.
