förlora (918)


Etymology

Derived from the past participle of Middle Low German vorlēsen, from Old Saxon farliosan, from Proto-West Germanic *fraleusan. For the derivation from the past participle compare also Afrikaans verloor.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

förlora (present förlorar, preterite förlorade, supine förlorat, imperative förlora)

  1. to lose (cause (something) to cease to be in one's possession or capability)
  2. to lose (a competition or a fight)
