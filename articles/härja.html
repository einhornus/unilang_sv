härja (28343)


Etymology

From Old Swedish hæria, from Old Norse herja, from Proto-Germanic *harjōną. Derived from *harjaz (see här).

Verb

härja (present härjar, preterite härjade, supine härjat, imperative härja)

  1. To wage war
  2. To ravage, to plunder
  3. to cause a stir
