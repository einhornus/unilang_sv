högskola (12453)


Noun

högskola c

  1. college (university); university college; an institution of higher education teaching undergraduates, but does not in general have the rights to issue graduate degrees, except for specified fields.

Usage notes

  • In practice, the term is also retained in some names of universities even after they were given permission to issue graduate degrees. See also the entry högskola in English Wikipedia.
