läsa (704)


Etymology

From Old Swedish læsa, from Old Norse lesa, from Proto-Germanic *lesaną, from Proto-Indo-European *les-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈlɛːˌsa/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: lä‧sa

Verb

läsa (present läser, preterite läste, supine läst, imperative läs)

  1. to read; to interpret written information
  2. to read; to make a study of (usually at a university)
  3. to read; to interpret a meaning
