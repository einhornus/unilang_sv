papper (1311)


Etymology

From late Old Norse pappir.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²papɛr/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

papper n

  1. paper (material for writing on)
  2. a sheet of paper
  3. a document
    ha sina papper i ordning
    have one's papers in order (i.e. passport, visa)
  4. (colloquial) a paper; a scientific (peer reviewed) article
