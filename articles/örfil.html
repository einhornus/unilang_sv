örfil (14273)


Etymology

From öra (“ear”) + fil. Compare German Ohrfeige.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): [œ́ːrfíːl]

Noun

örfil c

  1. A slap over the side of the head (or over the cheek).
    Synonyms: dadel, fikon, hurring, kindhäst, smällfikon
  2. (figurative) An insult, a humiliation.
  3. (Finland) cinnamon roll
    Synonyms: kanelbulle, kanelsnäcka
