avleda (16414)


Etymology

From av- + leda, like German ableiten.

Verb

avleda (present avleder, preterite avledde, supine avlett, imperative avled)

  1. to divert, drain, distract (water, attention or an attack)
    taskspelaren sökte avleda åskådarnas uppmärksamhet
    the jester sought to divert the spectators' attention
  2. (linguistics) to derive
