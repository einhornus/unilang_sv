livstid (3995)


Etymology

From liv + -s- + tid.

Noun

livstid c

  1. age (whole duration of a being), a lifetime
    Bach och Mozart fick inte uppleva jazzarrangemang av sina kompositioner under sina livstider.
    Bach and Mozart did not experience jazz arrangements of their compositions during their lifetimes.
