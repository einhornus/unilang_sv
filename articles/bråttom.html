bråttom (1681)


Etymology

From brått, neuter of bråd, + adverbial suffix -om.

Adverb

bråttom

  1. hurry, lack of time (you have bråttom, rather than being in a hurry)
    jag har bråttom ― I'm in a hurry
    nu är det bråttom med lanseringen
    now it's a rush with the launch
    Synonym: brådis (colloquial)

Usage notes

Note that in Swedish you have hurry 'ha bråttom', whereas in English you are in a hurry.
