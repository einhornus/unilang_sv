vintertid (80925)


Etymology

vinter (“winter”) + tid (“time”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /vɪntɛrˈtiːd/

Noun

vintertid c

  1. winter, wintertime (season, time of year)
  2. wintertime, without daylight saving time (standard time)
