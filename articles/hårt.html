hårt (629)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /hoːʈ/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective

hårt

  1. absolute indefinite neuter form of hård.

Adverb

hårt (comparative hårdare, superlative hårdast)

  1. in a hard manner; inflexibly, unyieldingly
  2. in a manner which demands a lot of effort to endure
  3. in a hard manner; severely, harshly
  4. forcefully
  5. tightly; fastened with great force
