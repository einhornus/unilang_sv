oljefat (82383)


Etymology

olja + fat, since 1755.

Noun

oljefat n

  1. an oil barrel; a barrel made from steel to contain oil to the amount of one barrel
  2. a steelpan, a steel drum; a percussion instrument from the West Indies, originally made from oil barrels
