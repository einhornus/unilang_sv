beräkna (18908)


Etymology

Probably from Middle Low German berēkenen, equivalent to be- + räkna. Cognate with Danish beregne, Norwegian Bokmål beregne, Norwegian Nynorsk berekne, berekna, Dutch berekenen, Low German beräknen, German berechnen, West Frisian berekkenje.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

beräkna (present beräknar, preterite beräknade, supine beräknat, imperative beräkna)

  1. (transitive) to calculate (to determine value)
    Synonym: räkna ut
