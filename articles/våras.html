våras (19101)


Etymology

From vår + -as.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²voːras/
  • Rhymes: -²oːras

Noun

våras

  1. adverbial genitive form of vår

Usage notes

  • Only found in the expression i våras (“last spring”).

Verb

våras (present våras, preterite vårades, supine vårats, imperative våras)

  1. to approach spring
  2. (figurative) to approach better times
