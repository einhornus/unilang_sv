sköt (606)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈɧøːt/

Noun

sköt c or n

  1. a fishnet for herring c
  2. (poetic) bosom, Contraction of sköte. n
    • 1804, Esaias Tegnér, Till min hembygd
      Var tanke i ditt sköt blir, som din himmel klar.
      Each thought in your bosom becomes, as your heaven clear.
