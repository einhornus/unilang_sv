svartis (84261)


Etymology 1

Black ice [1] on pavement
Black ice [2] on a lake

svart + is

Pronunciation 1

  • IPA(key): /²svartiːs/, [²svaʈiːs]

Noun

svartis c

  1. black ice, a thin barley visible layer of ice on roads and pavements
    Var försiktig, det var svartis när jag gick imorse.
    Be careful, there was black ice when I left this morning!
  2. congelation ice that looks black due to transparency

Etymology 2

A jealous person

Clipping of svartsjuk (“jealous”) + -is.

Pronunciation 2

  • IPA(key): /¹svartɪs/, [¹svaʈɪs]

Adjective

svartis (comparative mer svartis, superlative mest svartis)

  1. (colloquial) jealous
    Hon var svartis.
    She was jelous.
    Synonyms: svartsjuk, sotis (colloquial)

Declension

Invariable, only used predicatively.
