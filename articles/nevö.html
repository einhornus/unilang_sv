nevö (39584)


Etymology

Borrowed from French neveu, from Old French neveu, from Latin nepōs, nepōtem, from Proto-Italic *nepōts, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *népōts.

Noun

nevö c

  1. (somewhat formal) nephew (fraternal or sororal nephew)
