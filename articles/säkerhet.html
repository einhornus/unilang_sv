säkerhet (1205)


Etymology

From Old Swedish sikerhet, from Middle Low German sekerheit, akin to säker + -het.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

säkerhet c

  1. (uncountable) certainty
  2. (uncountable) security, safety (condition of not being threatened)
  3. a security (a guarantee for a loan)
