begrava (3616)


Etymology

From Proto-Germanic *bigrabanÄ…. Equivalent to be- + grav + -a.

Verb

begrava (present begraver, preterite begravde or begrov, supine begravt, imperative begrav)

  1. to bury; to perform a burial
  2. to bury; to hide or conceal; to cover with earth or large quantities of earth or snow
  3. to bury; to put an end to
