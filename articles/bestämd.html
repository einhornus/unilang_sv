bestämd (8628)


Etymology

past participle of bestämma.

Pronunciation

  • Audio

Adjective

bestämd

  1. (grammar) definite
    bestämd artikel = definite article
  2. fixed, determined; which is uniquely and unchangeably specified
  3. firm, unchanging; not prone to change opinions
  4. set, decided; which has been decided upon
    Tidpunkten är bestämd sedan länge, vi kan inte ändra den nu.
    The time has been set a long time ago, we cannot change that now.
  5. determined, measured
    Massan är bestämd noggrannare än någonsin förut
    The mass is determined with a greater accuracy than ever before.
