knacka (4654)


Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

knacka (present knackar, preterite knackade, supine knackat, imperative knacka)

  1. to knock; to rap one's knuckles against something, e.g. against a door
  2. (about an engine) to knock; to have the fuel ignite at the wrong time
  3. to tap; to make a sharp noise as if knocking on something
