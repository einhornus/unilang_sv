teoretisk (26178)


Etymology

teori + -isk

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /tɛʊˈreːtɪsk/

Adjective

teoretisk

  1. theoretic (concerned with theories or hypotheses rather than with practical matters).
  2. theoretic (existing only in theory, not proven in reality).
  3. theoretical (of or pertaining to theoretic studies).
