dittills (116791)


Adverb

dittills (not comparable)

  1. theretofore, until that time
    Han hade dittills aldrig kommit försent, men den morgonen ringde inte väckarklockan.
    He had never yet been late, but that morning the alarm clock didn't ring.

Usage notes

Keep in mind the distinction between dittills (to that point in time) and hittills (to this point in time).
