avgång (8951)


Etymology

From av- + gång.

Noun

avgång c

  1. departure; the act of leaving
    Tågets avgång är beräknad till klockan tio
    The departure of the train is estimated for ten o'clock
  2. resignation; the action of leaving a board, committee or other organization to which one has once been elected.
