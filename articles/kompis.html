kompis (543)


Etymology 1

Clipping of kompanjon + -is. Compare German Kumpel, Dutch kompel, Polish kumpel, and Spanish compa.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /¹kɔmpɪs/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

kompis c

  1. (informal) pal, friend, comrade, mate, playmate
    Synonyms: vän, polare (colloquial), kamrat (somewhat dated in this sense)

Etymology 2

Clipping of kompani + -is.

Noun

kompis ? (uncountable)

  1. (then colloquial, now dated) company

Usage notes

It was mostly (or perhaps only) used in the phrase i kompis (“together”), literally in company.
