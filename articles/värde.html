värde (3439)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /²vɛrdɛ/, [²væɖɛ]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Etymology

From Old Swedish værþe, which is derived from the Old Swedish noun værþ, which is cognate with the English noun worth and with the German noun Wert.[1] From Old Norse verðr (“worth”). Compare Norwegian verdi, Danish værdi.

Noun

värde n

  1. value (quality (positive or negative) that renders something desirable or valuable)
  2. value; degree of importance
  3. value; worth; amount (of money, goods or services) that is considered to be a fair equivalent for something else
  4. value; numerical quantity
