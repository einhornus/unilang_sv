valack (90346)


Etymology

Used since 1582 < German Wallach (“gelding”) < Walachei (“Wallachia”); probably because the practice of castrating horses spread to Europe from the East

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˌvaˈlak/

Noun

valack c

  1. a gelding
  2. a Wallachian, a person from Wallachia
