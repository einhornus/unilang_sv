meddela (2463)


Etymology

med + dela

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /meː(d)dela/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

meddela (present meddelar, preterite meddelade, supine meddelat, imperative meddela)

  1. to communicate, to notify ((transitive) To give (someone) notice of (something))

Usage notes

  • Recommendations against the use of this word in legal prose, together with suggested replacements, are found in Svarta listan : Ord och fraser som kan ersättas i författningsspråk (4th ed., 2011), published by the government of Sweden.
