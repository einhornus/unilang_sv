sommartid (48681)


Etymology

sommar (“summer”) + tid (“time”)

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /sɔmarˈtiːd/

Noun

sommartid c

  1. summer, summertime (season, time of year)
  2. summer time, daylight saving time (part of the year when clocks are advanced one hour ahead of standard time)
