anmälan (6787)


Etymology

anmäla + -an

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

anmälan c

  1. a registration (for participation)
    Anmälan om deltagande i mötet göres till Allmänna svenska folkskollärmötet, Stockholm.
    Registration of participation in the congress should be sent to the Public Swedish schoolteachers' congress, Stockholm.
  2. a report (to the police or similar authority)
    Då en rörelse upphör, skall anmälan härom göras till handelsregistret.
    When a business is closed down, a report of this should be made to the register of trade.
  3. a review or announcement (of a new book)
    Så skriver Gjörwell i sin anmälan i Stockholms lärda tidn. 2 mars:
    Thus writes Gjörwell in his review in Stockholm's scholarly journ. of March 2:
