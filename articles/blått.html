blått (5407)


Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /blɔtː/
  • Homophone: blott

Adjective

blått

  1. absolute indefinite neuter form of blå.

Noun

blått (no inflection)

  1. blue

Usage notes

Color nouns in Swedish are only marginally inflected in genitive (blåtts). The form is very seldom used and is generally proscribed.
