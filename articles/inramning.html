inramning (90929)


Etymology

rama in or inrama + -ning

Noun

inramning c

  1. framing (putting a picture in a frame)
  2. border, frame
  3. surrounding, context
    det gamla slottet med dess vackra inramning av skog och sjö
    the old castle and its beautiful surrounding of forest and lake
