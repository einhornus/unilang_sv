erkänna (2011)


Etymology

From German erkennen or Low German erkennen. Cognate with German erkende, Norwegian Bokmål erkjenne, Norwegian Nynorsk erkjenne, erkjenna, Dutch erkennen and Luxembourgish erkennen. Equivalent to er- + känna

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈeːrɕɛˌna/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb

erkänna (present erkänner, preterite erkände, supine erkänt, imperative erkänn)

  1. admit; to concede as true
  2. recognize; acknowledge the existence of
  3. recognize; realize or discover the nature of something
