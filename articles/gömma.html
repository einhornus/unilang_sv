gömma (1503)


Etymology

From Old Swedish gø̄ma, from Old Norse geyma, from Proto-Germanic *gaumijaną.

Pronunciation

  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun

gömma c

  1. place where things are hidden or stashed away

Verb

gömma (present gömmer, preterite gömde, supine gömt, imperative göm)

  1. to hide, to conceal
  2. (reflexive) to hide oneself
    Hunden gömde sig under sängen.
    The dog hid under the bed.
