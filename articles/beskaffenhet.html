beskaffenhet (80721)


Noun

beskaffenhet c

  1. character, property, nature
    Masugnarna indelas efter bränslets beskaffenhet i träkols- och koksmasugnar.
    Blast furnaces are categorized by the character of the fuel in charcoal and coke furnaces.

Usage notes

  • Recommendations against the use of this word in legal prose, together with suggested replacements, are found in Svarta listan : Ord och fraser som kan ersättas i författningsspråk (4th ed., 2011), published by the government of Sweden.
