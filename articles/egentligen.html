egentligen (631)


Etymology

egentlig + -en

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɛˈjɛntlɪ(ɡ)ɛn/, (colloquial) /ɛˈjɛŋklɪ(ɡ)ɛn/
  • Compare the colloquial pronunciation, where -änt becomes -enk, with äntligen and ordentlig.

Adverb

egentligen (not comparable)

  1. actually, in truth
    Jag ville egentligen gå på bio, men om du hellre stannar hemma gör jag det med.
    I actually wanted to go to the movies, but if you prefer to stay at home I’ll do so too.
  2. properly, formally, officially
    Det heter egentligen inte så.
    Officially that is not the correct term.
  3. technically, essentially
    Barr och löv är egentligen samma sak.
    Needle leaves and broad leaves are essentially the same thing.
