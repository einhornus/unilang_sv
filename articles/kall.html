kall (1983)


Etymology

From Old Swedish kalder, kaller, from Old Norse kaldr, from Proto-Germanic *kaldaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gel-.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /kal/
  • audio
    		(file)

Adjective

kall

  1. cold; having a low temperature.
  2. unfriendly, emotionally distant or unfeeling.
