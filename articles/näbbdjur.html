näbbdjur (87339)


Etymology

näbb (“beak”) + djur (“animal”). Calque of German Schnabeltier. Compare Danish næbdyr, Norwegian Bokmål nebbdyr.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /nɛb.jʉːr/

Noun

näbbdjur n

  1. platypus, duck-billed platypus; Ornithorhynchus anatinus
