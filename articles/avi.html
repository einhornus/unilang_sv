avi (28178)


Etymology

Clipping of avisering (“notification”).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ˈaˌviː/

Noun

avi

  1. a short message (physical or digital) from a postal operator or bank (that something needs to be collected)
Jag fick en avi i brevlådan om att jag har ett paket att hämta på snabbköpet.
I got a message in the letter box about a parcel I have to collect from the supermarket.
