skrot (9799)


Etymology

From Middle Low German schrôt, from Old Saxon skrōt. Compare German Schrot.

Noun

skrot c or n

  1. shot (small metal balls used as ammunition)
  2. scrap (junk) n
  3. a scrapyard, a junkyard c
