ungefär (979)


Etymology

Borrowed from German ungefähr (without intention).

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /ɵnjeˈfɛːr/, /ɵŋeˈfɛːr/, /ɵnjeˈfæːr/, /ɵŋɛˈfæːr/, /ɵŋjɛˈfæːr/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -æːr

Adverb

ungefär (not comparable)

  1. about, approximately
    Mannen är ungefär fyrtio år gammal.
    The man is approximately 50 years old.

Synonyms

  • cirka

Related terms

  • ungefärlig
  • ungefärligen
