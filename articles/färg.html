färg (2021)


Etymology

From Old Swedish færgha, from Middle Low German verwe or varwe, from Proto-Germanic *farwō. Cognate with German Farbe, Danish farve, Dutch verf and Norwegian farge.

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fɛrj/
  • audio
    		(file)

Noun

färg c

  1. color; the spectral composition of visible light
  2. color; perceived or named class of colors (1)
  3. color; human skin tone
  4. color; color television
  5. (physics) color; property of quarks
  6. (heraldry) color
  7. suit; card game category
  8. paint, dye, colorant
